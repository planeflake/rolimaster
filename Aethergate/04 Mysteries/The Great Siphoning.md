# The Great Siphoning

## Overview

The Great Siphoning is the hidden truth behind many small failures in [[Aethergate]]: pressure losses, unstable canisters, missing resonance, exhausted spellcasters, and old conduits that no longer match their maps.

On the surface, the city is suffering from theft.

Below the surface, something is learning to harvest mana before civilization can measure it.

## Connection to Absorption Arrays

Legal [[Mana Absorption Arrays]] collect loose ambient mana and leave the current intact.

Illegal arrays can leech from conduits.

The oldest secret arrays do something worse: they reveal that another intelligence was already drinking from the currents long before Aetherspark built the grid.

## Campaign Truth

Aetherspark's final designs were not meant to increase production.

They were diagnostic instruments disguised as harvesters.

Someone has found those designs, misunderstood or exploited them, and built modern siphon nets into the old routes beneath Cogmarket.

The danger is not new. [[The Last Draining]] proved that mana can be stolen from the world at a scale large enough to break history itself.
