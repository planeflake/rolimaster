# The Lost Cycles

## Overview

The Lost Cycles are stretches of history that cannot be reliably dated, audited, or reconstructed.

Some were lost to ordinary collapse, but at least one was broken by [[The Last Draining]], when the divine war emptied the world of usable mana and destroyed the institutions that preserved history.

## Campaign Truth

Not every missing record was burned, buried, or forgotten.

Some records were converted into Channeling during the divine war. Their absence is not silence; it is evidence of what the gods consumed.
