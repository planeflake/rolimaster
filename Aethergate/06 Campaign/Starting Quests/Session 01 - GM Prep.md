# Session 01 - GM Prep

## Starting State

Cycle 1127, [[The Day of Open Valves]].

The first full citywide Open Valves celebration in seven years is underway. Aethergate is bright, loud, relieved, and suspiciously proud of its surplus.

The opening incident is not an exploding canister. It is a hidden pipeline tap beneath [[The Smouldering Cog]] that spoofs a sensor so the line reports more mana than it actually carries.

## Clue Structure

### Easy Clues

- The tap is real and was installed beneath the tavern.
- Someone wanted the incident to look like crude pipeline theft.
- Pip delivered parts wrapped in blue wax paper.
- Bronn can tell the pipe reading and the pipe sound do not agree.

### Closer Inspection

- The pressure report was falsified upward.
- The tap did not simply steal mana; it changed what the system believed was still present.
- The altered sensor would hide losses elsewhere in the same line.
- The spoof burned out when festival pressure surged.

### Deep Clues

- The line was touched before the surface valve.
- The pattern resembles an illegal [[Mana Absorption Arrays|siphon net]] breathing in pulses.
- Someone is testing how to make the city's pipeline records lie.
- The apparent smuggling is camouflage for infrastructure manipulation.

## Likely Rolemaster Checks

- **Alertness / Perception:** notice frost, vibration, wrong whistle, or a person leaving.
- **Power Awareness:** sense wrong resonance or pre-valve loss.
- **Engineering / Technical Lore:** identify tap clamp, sensor tooth, spoofed pressure report.
- **Streetwise:** connect blue wax, Venn, Pip, and Cogmarket runners.
- **Interrogation / Lie Perception:** get useful truth from Pip without scaring him silent.
- **Public Speaking / Leadership:** calm the tavern during the pressure burst.
- **Stalk and Hide / Tracking:** follow the person who leaves during the confusion.

## Three Player Paths

### Work With Elara

The party helps [[Inspector Elara Voss]] secure the scene and compare official reports.

Lead: the sensor numbers point toward [[Valve Station 17]].

### Follow Pip

The party protects or pressures [[Pip Brasswhistle]] until he admits what he delivered and where.

Lead: blue wax packets, the old pressure hatch, and [[The Copper Steps]].

### Trust Bronn

The party listens to [[Bronn Gearwright]] and investigates the pipe behaviour instead of the obvious tap.

Lead: pre-valve resonance loss and a hidden route toward [[The Underworks]].
