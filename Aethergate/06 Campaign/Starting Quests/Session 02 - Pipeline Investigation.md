# Session 02 - Pipeline Investigation

## Premise

Following the incident at [[The Smouldering Cog]], the party investigates a series of minor mana thefts in Aethergate's Pipeline Quarter.

## Quest Givers

- [[Inspector Elara Voss]]
- [[Bronn Gearwright]]
- [[Mara Cogswain]]

## Key Discovery

The thefts appear to happen before mana reaches the registered surface conduits.

This should be impossible.

Bronn Gearwright believes it is possible only if someone has built an illegal [[Mana Absorption Arrays|Underworks Siphon Net]] into old pipework beneath the district.

## Locations

### [[Valve Station 17]]

A cramped pressure station where the official readings first become inconsistent.

### The Copper Steps

A stairway beneath Cogmarket used by pipeline workers and smugglers.

### Old Pressure Hatch

A sealed access point below [[The Smouldering Cog]].

## Clues

- Resonance loss before the valve
- Burn marks from non-standard siphon clamps
- Blue-white frost on copper fittings, suggesting an array is over-condensing ambient mana
- A scrap of blackened blue cloth
- A chalk mark showing a rat-like symbol, dismissed as gang graffiti
- A phrase overheard: "The Deep Route pays better."

## Complication

A surface cartel enforcer tries to scare the party away.

The enforcer does not know about the Skaven. They believe they are protecting a profitable supply line from independent smugglers.
