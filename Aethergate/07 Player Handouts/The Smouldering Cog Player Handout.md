# The Smouldering Cog

The Smouldering Cog is a famous tavern in Aethergate, built inside the shell of one of Aetherspark's earliest flying boat prototypes.

The old vessel never truly flew, but its brass ribs, suspended propellers, and soot-darkened boiler now form one of the city's warmest and strangest drinking halls.

Pipeline workers, artificers, airship crews, adventurers, and off-duty inspectors all gather beneath its curved hull.

The house stew is good.

The ale is strong.

The pipes in the walls glow orange when the city mana pressure rises.

And lately, they have been flickering.
