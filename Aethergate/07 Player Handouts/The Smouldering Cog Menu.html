House Notices

The boiler whistles when city pressure changes. Do not whistle back unless you know what you are doing.

Festival sparks are for drinks, lamps, and licensed performances only.

Mara reserves the right to refuse service to smugglers, prophets, unpaid inventors, and anyone carrying a leaking canister.

Ask at the bar for work, warnings, rumours, lost hats, or the name of someone who knows a better entrance below Cogmarket.
