# Valve Station 17

## Overview

Valve Station 17 is a cramped pressure station in the Pipeline Quarter where official readings first become inconsistent during the minor mana theft investigation.

It sits on a route between Cogmarket, [[The Convergence Works]], and older Underworks pipework.

## Appearance

The station is squat brick and brass with a round valve door marked **17** in chipped white paint.

Thick pipes enter at awkward angles. Small pressure bells hang under the eaves. A fenced yard holds tool lockers, spare gauges, and a locked stair descending to maintenance levels.

During the current crisis, blue-white frost sometimes gathers on copper fittings even when the station is warm.

## What Makes It Important

The gauges do not show an ordinary leak.

They show resonance loss before mana reaches the registered surface valve.

That should be impossible.

## Clues

- Burn marks from non-standard siphon clamps
- Frost on copper fittings
- Gauge needles settling half a beat late
- A hidden maintenance grate leading toward [[The Underworks]]
- Pressure logs that disagree with the official city report

## Campaign Uses

- Bronn Gearwright brings the party here to prove the theft is not normal.
- A surface cartel enforcer watches the station from a nearby roof.
- The pressure bells ring in the wrong order during the festival.
- The locked stair opens onto a route toward [[The Copper Steps]].
