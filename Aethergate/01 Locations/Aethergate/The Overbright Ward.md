# The Overbright Ward

## Overview

The Overbright Ward is a quarantined district on the outer limits of [[Aethergate]], sealed behind Arcane-fed containment wards and public fear.

It was once an industrial overflow district where mana storage, pressure venting, and experimental reclamation were handled too close to homes.

Now it is the city's most visible warning that mana surplus can be as dangerous as mana shortage.

## Brightrot

The district is infected with [[Brightrot]], a mana overdose disease caused by long exposure to oversaturated, decayed, or badly converted mana.

Some residents were evacuated.

Some refused to leave.

Some cannot leave.

## Containment

The ward is held by Arcane containment infrastructure that scrambles and bleeds excess mana before Brightrot can organize and spread.

Key defenses include:

- **Violet Breakwaters:** boundary wards that disrupt stable infection patterns.
- **Scramble Lamps:** lamps that flicker through impossible colors.
- **Null Gutters:** sealed runoff lines carrying contaminated condensate.
- **Glass Patrol Gates:** checkpoints with pressure masks and resonance testing.
- **Violet Bell Posts:** alarm stations that ring when containment pressure falls.

## Arcane Dependence

Arcane mana is dangerous, unstable, and politically dreaded.

It is also the only realm that reliably keeps Brightrot from cohering into a spreading pattern.

When Arcane reserves run critically low, the Overbright defenses become too orderly. The disease begins to move along pipes, breath, damp walls, stored mana, and living tissue.

## Current Tension

The city must sometimes choose between feeding Arcane to containment, holding it for [[Violet Protocol]], or using it to stabilize failing converters at [[The Convergence Works]].

The public is told the Overbright Ward is stable.

The public is not told how often that has nearly stopped being true.

## Campaign Uses

- Containment weakens on the same day as a major city festival.
- A Brightrot patient appears at the hospital after receiving Arcane-derived Essence.
- A cult sabotages Violet Breakwaters, believing Brightrot is ascension.
- The [[Ratio Office]] hides Arcane shortage numbers to prevent panic.
- The party must enter the ward to repair a Violet Bell Post before the next phase shift.
