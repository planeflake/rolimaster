# Aethergate Mana Cycle

## Overview

The Aethergate Mana Cycle is the civic calendar used to predict abundance, shortage, rationing, conversion, and decay risk across the city's mana systems.

It follows the shifting behaviour of [[The Nine Fonts]], which cycle between Essence, Mentalism, Channeling, and Arcane phases.

The cycle is predictable enough for planning and unreliable enough to frighten engineers.

## Cycle Stages

| Stage | Civic Effect | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| High Essence | Industry, hospital care, airship work, and repairs flourish | overconfidence |
| Falling Essence | Rationing begins and Heartline priority increases | black-market canisters |
| High Mentalism | Watch Net, archives, courts, and investigations sharpen | insomnia, shared dreams |
| Mentalism Conversion | excess Mentalism is converted to Essence | memory echoes and emotional bleed |
| High Channeling | rites, burial wards, oaths, sanctuary lamps, and plague barriers strengthen | religious leverage |
| Channeling Conversion | Channeling is converted only under pressure | protests, divine attention |
| Low Convergence | stable reserves are strained across all realms | crime, triage, hard rationing |
| Arcane Threshold | serious incidents may force Arcane conversion or weaken [[The Overbright Ward]] | catastrophic side effects |
| Arcane Surge | reserves can refill quickly under strict control | distortions, prophecy, old things stirring |

## Critical Points

### Low Convergence

Low Convergence is the most dangerous ordinary point in the cycle. Essence reserves are low, stored mana is often aging, and the city has already spent easier conversion sources.

If a hospital emergency, major fire, riot, plague outbreak, or pipeline sabotage occurs here, the [[Ratio Office]] must decide what can be sacrificed.

### Arcane Threshold

The Arcane Threshold is the city's break-glass moment.

If a serious incident occurs during the Threshold and clean Essence is unavailable, [[Violet Protocol]] permits Arcane-derived Essence to feed critical systems.

Everyone trains for this. Nobody wants it.

The Threshold also threatens [[The Overbright Ward]]. Its containment defenses need Arcane disruption to keep [[Brightrot]] from settling into a spreading pattern.

If the city must choose between the Heartline, the converters, and Overbright containment, the cycle has become a civic emergency.

## Campaign Uses

- A session begins during Falling Essence with district rationing already in effect.
- A cult chooses Low Convergence for sabotage because the city has fewer safe options.
- A public official hides that the city is closer to the Arcane Threshold than announced.
- The party must decide whether to support Channeling conversion or risk Violet Protocol.
