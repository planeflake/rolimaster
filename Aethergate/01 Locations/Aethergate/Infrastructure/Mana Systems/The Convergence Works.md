# The Convergence Works

## Overview

The Convergence Works is the main mana processing plant of [[Aethergate]], built into the foundations and lower chambers of [[Aetherspark's Tower]].

Nearly every major mana main in the city either passes through it, reports to it, or depends on its pressure calculations.

Most citizens call it Aetherspark's Heart.

Aetherspark's Heart feeds [[The Heartline]].

## Purpose

The Works receives incoming mana from the city's Font-fed mains, separates it by realm, stabilizes pressure, stores short-term reserves, converts mana when necessary, and routes priority supply to critical infrastructure.

Its most politically protected output is [[The Heartline]], the dedicated Essence pipeline serving the main hospital complex.

## Major Outputs

- **The Heartline:** Essence feed to the hospital complex.
- **Lamp Grid:** mixed civic Essence for lighting, lifts, pumps, heat, and public works.
- **Watch Net:** Mentalism feed for security, investigation, archives, and civic alarms.
- **Shrine Allotment:** Channeling feed for burial wards, oath courts, sanctuary lamps, plague barriers, and public rites.
- **Industrial Mains:** factories, foundries, docks, airship yards, and guild workshops.
- **Emergency Arcane Lock:** sealed feed opened only under crisis protocols.

## Aethersparkian Principle

Aetherspark designed civic mana systems to listen before they draw, regulate before they release, reclaim before wasting, and protect life before profit.

The Convergence Works embodies that doctrine at city scale. Every valve is paired with a gauge, every reservoir with a vent, and every converter with a brake.

## Control

The Works is jointly administered by the [[Mana Authority]], the [[Guild of Conduits]], the [[Ratio Office]], and the city's civic magistrates.

Hospital representatives hold emergency veto rights over any action that would reduce Heartline pressure below survival thresholds.

## Security

The public upper tower is ceremonial and bureaucratic. The lower Works are guarded, warded, and compartmentalized.

The [[White Line Wardens]] maintain a permanent post near the Heartline gates and converter halls.

## Campaign Uses

- A pressure decision made in the Works causes a district blackout but saves a hospital ward.
- A converter hall shows side effects from Mentalism-derived Essence before the [[Ratio Office]] admits conversion happened.
- Saboteurs target the Arcane Lock during Low Convergence.
- Old Aetherspark instruments beneath the Works point downward instead of toward the Fonts.
