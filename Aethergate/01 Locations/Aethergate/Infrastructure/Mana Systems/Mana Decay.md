# Mana Decay

## Overview

Stored mana does not last forever.

It loses coherence, drifts from its realm identity, gathers residue, and eventually becomes inefficient or dangerous.

This is why [[Aethergate]] cannot simply stockpile its way out of shortage.

## Decay Stages

| Stage | Condition | Common Use |
|---|---|---|
| Fresh | clean, bright, efficient | hospital, precision work, wards |
| Settled | safe but weaker | lamps, heat, pumps, civic devices |
| Stale | inefficient and side-effect prone | industrial use, emergency rationing |
| Soured | dangerous and unstable | restricted, condemned, or criminal use |
| Dead Mana | incoherent residue | disposal, cult interest, forbidden alchemy |

## Realm Decay

### Essence

Essence decays into pressure instability. Old Essence causes engine stutter, lamp flares, trembling surgical tools, and explosive canister ruptures.

### Mentalism

Mentalism decays into echo contamination. Old Mentalism causes memory bleed, false recognition, nightmares, paranoia, and devices that respond to thoughts no one meant to have.

### Channeling

Channeling decays into spiritual sediment. Old Channeling gathers intention, grief, oath, blessing, guilt, or divine attention.

### Arcane

Arcane does not decay normally. It curdles into impossibility.

Old Arcane mana may weigh the wrong amount, cast shadows before objects move, reverse small causes and effects, or whisper through sealed glass.

## Grades

- **First Draw:** direct current, used immediately.
- **Day Glass:** safe short-term storage.
- **Ward Grade:** fresh enough for hospital use.
- **Civic Grade:** lamps, pumps, heating, and ordinary devices.
- **Forge Grade:** industrial use only.
- **Grey Stock:** stale but legally usable during shortage.
- **Black Stock:** unregistered, old, mixed, or illegally stabilized.
- **Dead Draw:** officially condemned.

## Law

Every legal canister is stamped with draw date, realm, grade, and conversion origin.

Forgery of Ward Grade or Heartline supply marks is treated as a grave crime.

## Campaign Uses

- A desperate family buys stale Essence after a hospital refuses it.
- Black Stock is relabeled as Ward Grade and sold into a clinic.
- Soured Channeling creates a haunting around a storage vault.
- The city schedules [[The Day of Open Valves]] before surplus becomes dangerous.
