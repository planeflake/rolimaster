# Mana Conversion

## Overview

Mana conversion is the forced translation of one mana realm into another.

In [[Aethergate]], conversion most often means turning Mentalism, Channeling, or Arcane mana into Essence so the city can keep [[The Heartline]] and other critical systems alive during Essence shortages.

Conversion is never clean. It loses volume, leaves traces, and creates political, spiritual, and physical side effects.

## Conversion Ratios

Ratios vary by cycle, plant condition, and batch purity, but the usual civic estimates are:

| Source | Result | Typical Ratio | Notes |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Mentalism | Essence | 3 to 1 | Precise but echo-prone |
| Channeling | Essence | 5 to 1 | Stable but spiritually contested |
| Arcane | Essence | 2 to 1 | Powerful but dangerous |

## Realm Memory

Converted Essence behaves like Essence, but it still remembers what it was.

That memory appears as side effects when the mana is used for healing, devices, wards, spellwork, or storage.

## Mentalism-Derived Essence

Mentalism-derived Essence is precise and responsive. It is useful for prosthetics, nerve repair, diagnostics, surgical instruments, archives, and security devices.

Possible side effects:

- Patients hear fragments of other patients' thoughts.
- Prosthetics develop phantom habits from previous batches.
- Surgical lights show memories instead of shadows.
- Doctors dream about operations before they happen.
- Machines activate just before they are touched.
- Wards acquire an emotional tone shared by everyone nearby.

## Channeling-Derived Essence

Channeling-derived Essence is warm, stable, and effective in healing and preservation work, but it carries intention, blessing, grief, judgment, or divine attention.

Possible side effects:

- Patients wake with borrowed visions.
- Surgical tools hesitate or refuse certain cuts.
- Healed flesh carries faint marks of a faith the patient does not follow.
- Priests and spirits sense that sacred power has been stripped and repurposed.
- Blessings or obligations cling to the treatment site.
- A god, ancestor, saint, or local spirit notices repeated use.

## Arcane-Derived Essence

Arcane-derived Essence is efficient, potent, and unstable. It is reserved for catastrophe conditions.

Possible side effects:

- Healed tissue glows faintly during later Arcane surges.
- Time feels uneven during surgery.
- Rooms develop impossible acoustics, shadows, or angles.
- Patients remember alternate versions of their injuries.
- Converter gauges briefly show more output than input, then demand repayment later.
- Something beneath or beyond [[The Nine Fonts]] notices.

## Law and Conflict

The [[Mana Authority]] treats conversion as a necessary civic tool.

The [[Guild of Conduits]] treats it as ugly but measurable engineering.

Religious and civic opposition groups consider Channeling conversion sacrilege, Mentalism conversion a violation of the mind, and Arcane conversion an invitation to disaster.

## Campaign Uses

- A hospital batch is fresh and effective but carries disturbing Channeling side effects.
- The city quietly runs Mentalism conversion before announcing rationing.
- A converter accident creates Essence that behaves correctly but dreams.
- Arcane conversion saves the Heartline and wakes an old instrument below [[Aetherspark's Tower]].
