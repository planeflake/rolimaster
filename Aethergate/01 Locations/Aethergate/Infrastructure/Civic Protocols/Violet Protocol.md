# Violet Protocol

## Overview

Violet Protocol is the emergency law that permits Arcane mana to be converted into Essence for critical civic systems.

It exists because there are points in the [[Aethergate Mana Cycle]] when serious incidents leave the city with no humane alternative.

It is politically entangled with [[The Overbright Ward]], because the same Arcane reserves needed for emergency conversion also help keep [[Brightrot]] contained.

## Activation

Violet Protocol can be invoked only when critical infrastructure is at risk and safer Essence sources are unavailable.

Expected authorizations include:

- The [[Mana Authority]]
- A senior representative of [[The Convergence Works]]
- The [[Guild of Conduits]]
- The hospital charter authority for [[The Heartline]]
- A civic magistrate or emergency governor

In practice, disasters do not always wait for seals.

## Effects

Arcane-derived Essence is potent and efficient, but its side effects are dangerous.

It may create time slips, unstable healing, impossible spaces, prophecy dreams, spell distortions, or attention from forces beneath or beyond [[The Nine Fonts]].

## Public Knowledge

Most citizens know Violet Protocol exists.

Most citizens also believe it has never been used in their lifetime.

This is not necessarily true.

## Campaign Uses

- The party is present when Violet Protocol must be invoked without full authorization.
- A faction tries to force Violet Protocol to prove the converters are dangerous.
- Records show an old Violet activation that the city officially denies.
- The Heartline survives, but patients begin sharing impossible memories.
- Overbright containment drops because Arcane was diverted to emergency conversion.
