# The Heartline

## Overview

The Heartline is a dedicated Essence pipeline running from [[The Convergence Works]] to Aethergate's main hospital complex.

It feeds surgery theaters, healing wards, prosthetic workshops, preservation rooms, quarantine barriers, childbirth wards, and emergency triage stations.

No ordinary civic use has priority over the Heartline.

## Public Meaning

The line is one of the few pieces of infrastructure most Aethergaters speak of with something like reverence.

When lamps dim across a district so the Heartline can hold pressure, people grumble, but they understand.

## Protection

The Heartline is protected by law, oath, and force.

The [[White Line Wardens]] guard exposed valves, escort emergency canisters, protect repair crews, and seal Underworks routes if the line is threatened.

Damaging or siphoning from the Heartline is treated as attempted murder by infrastructure.

## Mana Supply

The Heartline prefers clean native Essence.

When Essence supply wanes, the city may feed the line with converted Essence from [[Mana Conversion]]. These emergency batches keep the hospital alive, but they carry side effects from their original realm.

## Failure Signs

- Surgical lights flicker from white to grey, gold, or violet.
- Pressure bells ring without visible leaks.
- Preservation rooms frost at the door seams.
- Prosthetic tuning tables hum before touched.
- Patients in separate wards report the same dream.

## Campaign Uses

- The party must guard a repair team entering old pipework beneath Cogmarket.
- A hidden siphon net draws tiny amounts from the line without triggering official alarms.
- A hospital ward must choose between stale native Essence and fresh converted Essence.
- Protesters block a converter station that is feeding the line during a shortage.
