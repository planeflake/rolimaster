# White Line Barracks

## Overview

White Line Barracks is the fortified post of the [[White Line Wardens]], positioned between [[Aetherspark's Tower]] and the [[Ninefold Infirmary]].

The barracks protects the nearest Heartline valves, converter approaches, emergency canister routes, and hospital-facing tram entrances.

## Appearance

The building is compact, clean, and severe.

White enamel bands mark the walls. Copper pressure bells hang above the main gate. A red-painted conduit trace crosses the paving outside, warning citizens not to block the route.

The yard holds emergency canister carts, polearm racks, pressure masks, and drill posts shaped like illegal siphon clamps.

## Public Role

- Dispatch Heartline escorts
- Guard hospital valve crews
- Store emergency Essence canisters
- Train Wardens against siphon sabotage
- Process infrastructure-security arrests

## Current Tension

During [[The Day of Open Valves]], the barracks is understaffed. Too many Wardens are assigned to festival release arches, crowd safety, and public pressure posts.

## Campaign Uses

- The party is deputized here before entering a threatened maintenance route.
- A stolen Warden pressure mask turns up near Cogmarket.
- The barracks pressure bell rings while every visible gauge remains normal.
- A Warden asks the party to carry a sealed canister to the hospital without attracting attention.
