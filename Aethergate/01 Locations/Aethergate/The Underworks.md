# The Underworks

## Overview

The Underworks are the abandoned maintenance tunnels beneath [[Aethergate]].

They predate the modern city grid and include old conduits, failed reservoirs, drainage channels, sealed machine rooms, and forgotten experimental shafts from Aetherspark's early construction period.

## Public Knowledge

Most citizens know the Underworks exist, but assume they are unsafe, flooded, or sealed.

Pipeline workers sometimes enter them for emergency repairs.

Smugglers enter them far more often.

## Connection to The Smouldering Cog

A pressure hatch beneath [[The Smouldering Cog]] connects to an old maintenance shaft.

The tavern's owner, [[Mara Cogswain]], knows the hatch exists but claims it has been sealed for years.

It has not.

## Features

- Rusted mana pipes
- Old clockwork regulators
- Broken pressure gauges
- Unmapped tunnels
- Illegal siphon clamps
- Hidden cartel caches
- Strange claw marks near deeper passages

## Secrets

The deepest tunnels connect to routes that should not exist on any city plan.

Some of those passages lead toward the first hints of [[The Hidden Skaven]].
