# Aethergate

> Where Magic Runs Like Clockwork

## Overview

Aethergate is the primary campaign city of Mana World and the greatest living monument to [[Aetherspark, Architect of the Currents|Aetherspark's]] mana infrastructure.

Its daily life depends on measured pressure, rationed supply, legal conversion, guarded pipelines, and the civic belief that mana cannot be wasted.

## Current Campaign Pressure

Minor mana thefts in Cogmarket are beginning to reveal deeper failures in the city's measurements.

If mana is being removed before it reaches registered surface conduits, Aethergate's rationing system, hospital supply, and public trust are all at risk.
