# Mana Economy

Mana is harvested, refined, transported, and regulated.

Most civic mana reaches cities through conduits, reservoirs, and pressure valves maintained by guild engineers. In [[Aethergate]], the largest mains converge at [[The Convergence Works]], where mana is measured, stored, converted, and routed.

Smaller quantities are collected by licensed [[Mana Absorption Arrays]], which draw loose ambient mana from the air and condense it into liquid or gas.

Liquid mana is easier to store, tax, and transport. Gaseous mana is harder to stabilize and is therefore tightly regulated, especially after the spread of black-market mana inhalers in [[Aethergate]].

The official economy depends on the idea that mana can be measured at the Fonts, measured again at the valves, and accounted for in between. Anything that removes mana before it reaches registered surface conduits threatens the entire system.

Stored mana also suffers [[Mana Decay]], which means surplus must be rotated, spent, reclaimed, converted, or released through civic events such as [[The Day of Open Valves]].
