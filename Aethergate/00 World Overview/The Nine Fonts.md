# The Nine Fonts

Nine great leyline nexuses power civilization.

Each Font cycles between Essence, Mentalism, Channeling, and Arcane phases. These cycles shape how mana behaves in the air, in conduits, in spellwork, and inside [[Mana Absorption Arrays]].

Most people believe the Fonts are the beginning of the mana currents.

They are only the visible beginning.
