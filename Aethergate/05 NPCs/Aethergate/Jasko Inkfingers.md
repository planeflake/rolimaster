# Jasko Inkfingers

## Overview

Jasko Inkfingers is a tiefling pamphleteer handing out doomsday fliers around Cogmarket.

Most people laugh at him.

Some of his details are wrong in the useful direction.

## Qualities

- Notices patterns others dismiss
- Brave enough to be mocked publicly
- Knows which officials avoid which questions
- Has an archive of old public notices

## Flaws

- Convinced every answer proves the worst possible theory
- Cannot separate fact from omen when frightened
- Agitates crowds by accident
- Thinks being ignored makes him more correct

## Rolemaster Notes

Suggested profession: Seer, Mystic, or Dabbler.

Useful skill areas: Research, Public Speaking, Divination Lore, Streetwise, Observation, Runes or Symbol Lore.

In play, Jasko should provide noisy clues, wrong conclusions, and one uncomfortable true detail.

## Campaign Use

Jasko claims the city is spending its last breath. He has a copied pressure correction showing Arcane reserves changed from "comfortable" to "adequate."
