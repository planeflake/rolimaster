# Rusk Brinehook

## Overview

Rusk Brinehook is an orc airship captain from the Glass Sea routes, drinking in [[The Smouldering Cog]] while his crew waits out festival traffic.

He knows harbors, cargo lies, and the sound of a crew member trying to look innocent.

## Qualities

- Loyal to his crew
- Practical under pressure
- Honest when bribery would be easier
- Knows Tideglass cargo habits

## Flaws

- Loud when subtlety would help
- Quick to assume officials are lying
- Protective enough to obstruct investigations
- Enjoys frightening people who underestimate him

## Rolemaster Notes

Suggested profession: Rogue or Fighter with Mariner training.

Useful skill areas: Sailing, Leadership, Trading, Region Lore, Perception, Brawling.

In play, Rusk can connect the party to Tideglass rumours and smuggling routes without being part of the tap.

## Campaign Use

Rusk saw a blue-scarved contact speaking with a dock runner before the festival rush.
