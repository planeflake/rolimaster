# Lira Copperquill

## Overview

Lira Copperquill is a gnome entertainer who plays the Broken Propeller Stage at [[The Smouldering Cog]].

She opens the party's first night with a festival toast during [[The Day of Open Valves]].

## Qualities

- Quick read of a room
- Kind to nervous strangers
- Excellent memory for faces
- Can make a joke sound like a warning

## Flaws

- Cannot resist a dramatic pause
- Hides fear behind patter
- Owes small debts to too many people
- Assumes every secret wants an audience

## Rolemaster Notes

Suggested profession: Bard.

Useful skill areas: Public Speaking, Play Instrument, Acting, Social Awareness, Streetwise, Perception.

In play, Lira is best used for social clues, crowd control, rumours, and drawing attention away from someone else.

## Campaign Use

Lira sees who leaves during the pressure-tap incident because she is already watching the room from the stage.
