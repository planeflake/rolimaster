# Bronn Gearwright

## Overview

Master Engineer Bronn Gearwright is a senior pipeline engineer in Aethergate's Guild of Conduits.

He has spent forty years listening to pipes and claims he can tell the difference between pressure loss, sabotage, and "something drinking from the line."

Most people think he is being dramatic.

He is not.

## Appearance

- Broad dwarf
- Burn-scarred hands
- Copper ear-horn used for listening to conduits
- Beard tied with brass pressure tags

## What He Knows

- The recent losses do not behave like normal thefts.
- Some conduits arrive under pressure but with reduced resonance.
- A few readings suggest mana is being pulled away before reaching the surface lines.
- The pattern resembles an illegal [[Mana Absorption Arrays|siphon net]] breathing in pulses.
- The old schematics beneath [[The Smouldering Cog]] do not match modern maps.

## Qualities

- Hears tiny pressure changes
- Honest when silence would be safer
- Knows old pipe maps by scar and habit
- Protective of younger engineers

## Flaws

- Assumes everyone else should hear what pipes are saying
- Drinks when officials ignore him
- Hates polished explanations
- More stubborn than diplomatic

## Rolemaster Notes

Suggested profession: Alchemist, Dabbler, or non-spell engineer with strong Technical skills.

Useful skill areas: Engineering, Metalcraft, Power Awareness, Perception, Region Lore, Trap Detection, Public Speaking when angry.

In play, Bronn can distinguish theft, pressure loss, sensor spoofing, and pre-valve resonance loss.

## Useful Dialogue

> "Pipes don't lie. People lie. Pipes scream."

> "If mana goes missing after the valve, that's theft. If it goes missing before the valve, that's a nightmare."
