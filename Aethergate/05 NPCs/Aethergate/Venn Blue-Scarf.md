# Venn Blue-Scarf

## Overview

Venn Blue-Scarf is the cautious contact linked to suspicious deliveries around Cogmarket.

They wear a blue scarf, keep one cheek partly hidden, and let others assume the burn scar is the most interesting thing about them.

## Qualities

- Patient observer
- Changes posture and voice easily
- Knows how to disappear into crowds
- Understands enough pipeline slang to fake confidence

## Flaws

- Overconfident in disguise work
- Leaves repeated blue-wax packaging habits
- Distrusts allies before enemies
- Panics when plans become loud

## Rolemaster Notes

Suggested profession: Magent or Rogue.

Useful skill areas: Disguise, Stalking and Hiding, Streetwise, Pick Locks, Lie Perception, Technical Lore.

In play, Venn should look like a smuggler while pointing toward someone with better sensor knowledge.

## Campaign Use

Venn handled deliveries used to make the tap look like ordinary theft. They may not know who designed the sensor spoof.
