Pip Brasswhistle

Young halfling runner with an oversized coat

Role
frightened witness
Rolemaster
Rogue or Thief
Use
delivery trail
Knows
blue wax and pressure hatch

Qualities

  • Fast on rooftops and under tables
  • Knows Cogmarket backways
  • Wants to do the right thing
  • Remembers wrappers, smells, and delivery marks

Flaws

  • Lies from fear, not skill
  • Touches things he should leave alone
  • Thinks running counts as a plan
  • Desperate to seem useful

Rolemaster Hooks

Stalk and Hide, Tumbling, Streetwise, Pick Pockets, Perception, Running, Lie Perception.

Clue Use

Pip delivered tap parts and blue-waxed packets. He hid a small brass sensor tooth in the Engine Shrine without knowing what it does.

Voice

"I didn't steal it. I just borrowed it before someone worse than me could lose it."
