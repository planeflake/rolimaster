# Mixed Men

## Summary

Mixed Men descend from unions between Common Men and High Men. They sit between the two in build, lifespan, social position, and racial modifiers.

## Typical Cultures

Mixed Men may not use Subterranean or Barbarian subcultures.

## Mana World Notes

Mixed Men are a strong fit for Aethergate's social borderlands, especially families caught between noble descent and common civic life.
