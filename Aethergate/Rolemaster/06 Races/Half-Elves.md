# Half-Elves

## Summary

Half-Elves are people of mixed Elven and Mannish ancestry. In the Shadow World, Half-Elven identity can also describe the Ta-Lairi, five related peoples: Eritari, Kytaari, Punkari, Sulini, and Vorloi.

Half-Elves must choose early in life whether they will follow mortality or immortality. Mortal Half-Elves tend to live among Men, especially [[High Men]], while immortal Half-Elves follow the ways of [[Elves]].

## Physical Description

- **Build:** Thinner than Men but stouter than Elves. Males average 190 pounds; females average 135 pounds.
- **Coloring:** Fair skin, gray eyes, and usually brown or black hair.
- **Endurance:** Greater stamina than most Men, but less than Elves. Half-Elves need only four hours of rest each day, sleeping if mortal and meditating if immortal.
- **Height:** Males average 6'3"; females average 5'11".
- **Life span:** Mortal Half-Elves usually live 250-500 years.
- **Resistance:** +50 RR versus disease. Resistant to natural cold, with +5 DB and RR versus cold attacks.
- **Special ability:** Dark Vision. Half-Elves see perfectly up to 500' in moonlight or starlight, perfectly up to 50' in low light, and reasonably well up to 100' in low light.

## Culture Modifiers

### Languages

- **Starting Languages:** Grey-elvish (S8/W6), Common-speech (S8/W6), High-elvish (S6/W5).
- **Allowed Adolescent Development:** Grey-elvish (S10/W10), Common-speech (S10/W10), High-elvish (S10/W10), High-speech (S10/W10), Elvish (S10/W10).

### Social Notes

- **Prejudices:** Half-Elves instinctively hate Orcs. They also feel a strong affinity for High Men, especially if they choose mortality.
- **Religious attitudes:** Quiet and personal, resembling Elven meditation more than organized mortal ritual.
- **Preferred professions:** No profession is especially favored or forbidden.
- **Special skills:** Everyman: None. Restricted: None.
- **Typical cultures:** Any culture template that fits the character's heritage and upbringing.

## Character Concepts

Half-Elf characters often stand between worlds. They may feel blessed by a wider inheritance or burdened by never fitting wholly into one people. Their mortal or immortal choice can shape where they live, who claims them, and what kind of future they believe is open to them.

## Background Options

Half-Elves receive four background options.

- **Extra Languages:** North-speech (S6/W6), Plains-speech (S6/W6), Wood-speech (S6/W6).
- **Extra Money:** Coins, gems, or jeweled items.
- **Special Items:** All special items are available, often made of finely wrought metal where appropriate.
- **Talents:** All are available.

## Variants

### Eritari

Eritari descend from intermingling between Elves and Thesians, or possibly the ancient Jinteni. They are tall, lithe, brown-eyed, and brown-skinned, with communities in Rhon, Thuul, Chaal-chu, and Arlandia.

Eritari need only four hours of rest per day and usually live about 200 years. They receive +5 DB and RR versus heat attacks and share the Half-Elven gift of Dark Vision. They tend to favor Thesians over other Mannish peoples and may use Littoral, Rural, or Woodfolk cultures.

### Kytaari

Kytaari resemble Iylari Elves and live in the cold lands of the Mur Fostisyr and Kalitaal. They are tall, slender, fair-skinned, and usually blonde, with blue or green eyes.

Kytaari need only four hours of rest per day and usually live 150-300 years. They are immune to natural cold, have Dark Vision, and possess Solar Renewal, recovering 1 PP per level per full hour spent resting in direct sunlight. They usually use the Arctic culture template.

### Punkari

Punkari descend from Elven and Y'nari or Laan ancestry. They are more powerfully built than most Half-Elves and are known for clan-scale societies, anarchic habits, and a harsh distrust of outsiders.

Punkari need only four hours of rest per day and usually live 200-300 years. They receive +5 DB and RR versus both heat and cold attacks and share the Half-Elven Dark Vision. Their culture template depends heavily on where their clan lives.

### Sulini

Sulini are tall, slender, fair Half-Elves descended from Erlini and a fair mortal people, most likely Talath or Lydians. Most live in fishing settlements along the coasts of Mythenis and Jaiman.

Sulini need only four hours of rest per day and usually live 200-300 years. They receive +5 DB and RR versus both heat and cold attacks and share the Half-Elven Dark Vision. They usually use Arctic or Littoral cultures.

### Vorloi

Vorloi, also called Tree Spirits, are a strange Half-Elven people of the Isle of Jade. They are short, pale, sharp-featured, and marked by long pointed ears, fangs, claw-like nails, and delicate hands.

Vorloi need only four hours of rest per day and often live 600 years or more. They receive +5 DB and RR versus both heat and cold attacks and share the Half-Elven Dark Vision. They usually use the Sylvan culture template, and Animists are common among them.

## Mana World Notes

Half-Elves are a strong fit for [[Aethergate]] cosmopolitan lineages, mixed Font inheritances, and border families between mortal and Elven communities. Their choice between mortal and immortal ways can be tied to exposure to the [[The Nine Fonts|Nine Fonts]], family oaths, or the expectations of old Elven houses.
