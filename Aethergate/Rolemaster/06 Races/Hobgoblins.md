# Hobgoblins

## Summary

Hobgoblins are larger, stronger Goblinoids who often dominate Goblin groups. They tolerate daylight better than Goblins, though direct sun still hinders them.

## Languages

Starting Languages: Goblin (S8/W6), optional Common-speech (S6/W4).

Allowed Adolescent Development: Common-speech (S8/W6), Goblin (S10/W8), Orcish (S4/W4).

## Mechanical Notes

- Dark vision in starlight, moonlight, and limited non-magical darkness.
- Sunlight vulnerability: -25 in direct sunlight.
- Everyman skills: Mining, Metal-craft.

## Typical Cultures

[[Subterranean Culture]] or [[Mining Culture]].

## Mana World Notes

Useful as exile-warriors, undercity bosses, or disciplined tunnel fighters who can operate at the edge of surface society.
