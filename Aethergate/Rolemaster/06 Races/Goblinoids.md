# Goblinoids

## Summary

Goblinoids are a family of underground peoples. The source groups Goblins, Hobgoblins, Kobolds, and Murlogi together, but each has its own racial information table and now has its own page.

## Races

- [[Goblins]]
- [[Hobgoblins]]
- [[Kobolds]]
- [[Murlogi]]

## Typical Cultures

[[Subterranean Culture]] is the default fit. [[Mining Culture]] can fit some Hobgoblin communities or Goblinoid groups with stronger craft and tunnel-building traditions.

## Mana World Notes

Separate hostile stereotype from playable culture when adapting; useful for undercity, borderland, and factional conflict notes.
