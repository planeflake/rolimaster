# Laan

## Summary

Laan are a prominent mortal Shadow World race known as warriors, leaders, and empire-builders. In Jaiman they are known as the Zori.

## Typical Cultures

Urban or Rural templates are common, depending on settlement. Laan are presented as advanced, ambitious, and civilized.

## Mana World Notes

Laan can map to old imperial bloodlines, city-state founders, or proud mortal houses with ancient Font claims.
