# High Men

## Summary

High Men are taller, longer-lived, and more aristocratic than Common Men. They are associated with leadership, empire-building, and elevated social status.

## Languages

Starting Languages: High-speech (S8/W6), Common-speech (S8/W6), Grey-Elvish (S6/W6), High-Elvish (S2/W2).

Allowed Adolescent Development: High-speech (S10/W10), Common-speech (S10/W10), Grey-Elvish (S8/W8), High-Elvish (S6/W6), Hill-speech (S6/W6), Sea-speech (S8/W8), Small-speech (S6/W6), Plains-speech (S6/W6).

## Typical Cultures

High Men may not use Subterranean, Sylvan, or Barbarian subcultures.

## Mana World Notes

High Men can become old ruling lineages, Font-blooded noble houses, or imperial families whose authority is contested in Aethergate.
