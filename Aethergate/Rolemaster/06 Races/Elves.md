# Elves

## Summary

Elves are immortal, graceful, perceptive, and magically resonant. The source consolidates RMFRP Wood, Grey, and High Elves with several Shadow World Elven peoples: Dyari, Erlini, Linaeri, Loari, and Shuluri.

## Variants

- **Wood Elves:** Rustic in presentation but culturally sophisticated; strongly tied to music, woods, forests, and mountains.
- **Grey Elves:** A distinct RMFRP Elven branch covered separately by the source.
- **High Elves:** A powerful and elevated RMFRP Elven branch covered separately by the source.
- **Dyari, Erlini, Linaeri, Loari, Shuluri:** Shadow World Elven peoples. Linaeri, Loari, and Dyari are closely related as Iylar.

## Physical Notes

Elves are tall, slender, long-lived to the point of immortality, and do not sleep in the ordinary mortal way. They recover through meditative trance and have strong sensory gifts.

## Mechanical Notes

- Elves are resistant to disease.
- Wood Elves have cold resistance and strong night vision short of absolute darkness.
- Elves have strong hearing; Wood Elves gain additional hearing-linked and performance-adjacent benefits in the source.
- Attribute bonuses make many Elves broadly capable, though the source notes some profession tension around Self-Discipline.
- Wood Elves are especially suitable as Bards, Dabblers, or stealth-capable characters.

## Culture Notes

Elven religion is informal and often expressed through communal ritual and meditation. The source stresses ancient enmity with Orcs and Dragons, with additional tensions toward Dwarves, Men, and intruders into Sylvan lands.

## Typical Cultures

- Wood Elves: [[Sylvan Culture]] or [[Aerial Culture]].
- Other Elven variants should be assigned by settlement and Mana World adaptation.

## Character Hooks

- A Wood Elf musician follows a song-fragment connected to a damaged Font.
- An immortal envoy comes to Aethergate because mortal politics are disturbing an old Elven pact.
- A Shadow World-inspired Elven lineage seeks proof that its people remember a pre-cycle truth.

## Mana World Notes

Elves should be strongly connected to long mana memory. Their trance-rest can become a way of listening to past Font cycles, and each Elven branch can be mapped to different relationships with Essence, Mentalism, Channeling, or Arcane mana.
