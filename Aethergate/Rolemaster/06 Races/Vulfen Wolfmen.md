# Vulfen Wolfmen

## Summary

Vulfen, also called Wolfmen, are lupine humanoids suited to mobile, predatory, and borderland roles.

## Languages

Starting Languages: Common-speech (S8/W6), plus either Plains-speech (S6/W6) or Wood-speech (S6/W6), depending on native environment.

Allowed Adolescent Development: Common-speech (S10/W8), either North-speech (S6/W6) or South-speech (S6/W6), and either Plains-speech (S8/W8) or Wood-speech (S8/W8), depending on native environment.

## Mana World Notes

Could serve as fierce rural or sylvan pack peoples, with a strong tie to predatory rites and borderland fear.
