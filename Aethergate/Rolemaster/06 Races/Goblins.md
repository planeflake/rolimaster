# Goblins

## Summary

Goblins are crude, clever underground folk with a strong mechanical streak. They are nocturnal, live in lairs and warrens, and suffer badly in daylight.

## Languages

Starting Languages: Goblin (S8/W6), optional Common-speech (S6/W4).

Allowed Adolescent Development: Common-speech (S8/W6), Goblin (S10/W8), Orcish (S4/W4).

## Mechanical Notes

- Dark vision in starlight, moonlight, and limited non-magical darkness.
- Sunlight vulnerability: -75 to all activities in daylight.
- Everyman skills: Mining, Metal-craft.
