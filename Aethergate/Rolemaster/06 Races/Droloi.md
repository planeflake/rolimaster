# Droloi

## Summary

Droloi are an unsettling Shadow World race with a scattered presence and no major population center. Their four eyes, pale leathery skin, claws, and hairless bodies make them appear alien to many other peoples.

## Variants

None listed in this source.

## Physical Notes

Droloi are humanoid, short to medium height, lightly built, clawed, and unusually adapted to darkness. They have pale leathery skin, dark protruding eyes arranged in two pairs, and no hair.

## Mechanical Notes

- Strong dark vision, including excellent sight in non-magical darkness.
- Leathery skin provides natural armor.
- Claws allow Medium Claw unarmed attacks.
- Notable tolerance for cold attacks, though not against below-freezing extremes.
- The source frames them as especially workable Fighters because of Strength and Constitution.

## Culture Notes

Droloi live in small groups across Kulthea. The source emphasizes their outsider status more than a single dominant society.

## Typical Cultures

Likely fits include [[Subterranean Culture]], [[Mining Culture]], or isolated [[Rural Culture]], depending on where a community is placed.

## Character Hooks

- A Droloi scout leaves a hidden settlement to learn why local mana has begun to darken.
- A Droloi fighter seeks acceptance in Aethergate despite the unease their appearance causes.

## Mana World Notes

In Mana World, Droloi could be descendants of communities altered by a deep Font cycle, especially in caverns where mana pools without sunlight. They make a strong fit for mysterious but not automatically villainous underground contacts near Aethergate.
