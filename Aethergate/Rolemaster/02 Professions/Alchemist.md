# Alchemist

## Summary

Alchemists are pure Essence spell users skilled at relating magic to the physical universe. They manipulate matter, shape items, and imbed spells from all three realms into physical objects, storing power for later use.

## Classification

## Spell Development

The Alchemy Companion lists Alchemist spell-list development as `1/*`. The core concept assumes the Alchemist can shape and store power, but still needs access to the spell being imbedded, either by knowing it personally or by using another caster, rune, or equivalent source.

## Training Packages

See [[Training Package Index]]. Alchemists strongly suit scholarly, craft, guild, merchant, apothecary, and laboratory-focused packages.

## Mana World Notes

In [[Aethergate]], Alchemists are indispensable to mana-rationed society because they turn fleeting Font access into durable tools. They craft warded locks, treated conduits, stabilizing reagents, charged crystals, calibrated lenses, and emergency items that can carry magic through a dry part of the cycle.

Alchemists are watched closely by guilds, customs offices, temples, and criminal brokers. A licensed Alchemist can keep a district alive during rationing; an unlicensed one can hide dangerous power in ordinary objects.

## Source Reference

The core Alchemist appears in *Alchemy Companion* section 6.1, page 57. Variant Alchemist professions begin in section 6.2 and include Adept, Royal Alchemist, Inorganic Alchemist, Organic Alchemist, Evil Alchemist, Thaumaturge, Theurgist, and others.

## Typical Abilities

- Enchantment, material analysis, item creation, imbedding, and prepared magical solutions.

## Situation Examples

- **Strange relic:** Identifies materials and tests how mana has been bound into the object.
- **Fortified gate:** Prepares an item or compound suited to bypassing the obstacle.
- **Long expedition:** Equips the group with specialized tools before departure.
