# Bard

## Summary

Bards combine Mentalism with arms training. Their magic focuses on sound, lore, performance, and the use of items.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Semi
- **Realm:** [[Mentalism]]
- **Prime stats:** Memory and Presence
