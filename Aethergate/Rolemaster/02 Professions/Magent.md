# Magent

## Summary

Magents are semi spell users who combine the realm of Mentalism with the realm of Arms. Their base lists support information gathering, subterfuge, and stealthy missions.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Semi
- **Realm:** [[Mentalism]]
- **Prime stats:** Reasoning and Presence
