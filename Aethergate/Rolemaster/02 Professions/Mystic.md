# Mystic

## Summary

Mystics are hybrid spell users who combine Essence and Mentalism. Their magic emphasizes subtle misdirection, personal illusion, and the alteration of matter.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Hybrid
- **Realms:** [[Essence]] and [[Mentalism]]
- **Prime stats:** Empathy, Presence, and Self Discipline

## Skill Notes

- **Everyman skills:** Time Sense, Adrenal Stabilization, Magic Ritual, Spell Mastery, Meditation
- **Occupational skills:** None
- **Restricted skills:** Channeling

## Spell Lists

- [[Confusing Ways]]: Affects a target's senses in varied ways.
- [[Gas Alteration]]: Manipulates and controls gases.
- [[Hiding]]: Makes things harder to detect, including through invisibility.
- [[Liquid Alteration]]: Manipulates and controls liquids.
- [[Mystical Change]]: Creates illusions on the caster.
- [[Solid Alteration]]: Manipulates and controls solids.

## Spell Development

Base lists cost `3/3/3`. Open-list costs progress from `4/4/4` to `6/6/6`, `8/8`, and `12`. Closed-list costs progress from `4/4/4` to `6/6/6`, `8/8`, `10/10`, and `25`.

The first five lists developed in a level use normal cost. Lists 6-10 cost `x2`, and later lists cost `x4`.
