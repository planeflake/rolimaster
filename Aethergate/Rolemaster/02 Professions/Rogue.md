# Rogue

## Summary

Rogues are flexible non-spell users who balance arms training with thief-like skills. Their costs sit between the Fighter and Thief specializations.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** None
- **Realm:** None
- **Prime stats:** Agility and Strength

## Profession Bonuses

| Area | Bonus |
|---|---:|
| Armor Group | +5 |
| Athletic - Gymnastic | +5 |
| Awareness Group | +5 |
| Body Development | +5 |
| Combat Maneuvers | +5 |
| Subterfuge Group | +10 |
| Weapon Group | +15 |

## Skill Notes

- **Everyman skills:** Duping, Lock Lore, choice of one Situational Awareness skill, and either Boxing or Tackling
- **Occupational skills:** None
- **Restricted skills:** None

## Spell Development

The Rogue has no base lists. Open-list costs range from `15` for levels 1-5 to `75` for levels 21+, while closed-list costs range from `25` to `125`.

## Training Packages

See [[Training Package Index]]. Typical packages in the sourcebook are [[Burglar]], [[City Guard]], [[Scout]], and [[Soldier]].

## Source Reference

The complete skill-category development-point table is on sourcebook page 94.

## Typical Abilities
- Flexible weapons, stealth, practical skills, and adaptable problem-solving.

## Situation Examples
- **Caravan attack:** Shifts between skirmishing, guarding civilians, and flanking.
- **Locked warehouse:** Scouts the site and chooses a practical way inside.
- **Tense meeting:** Reads the room and keeps a weapon-ready exit plan.
