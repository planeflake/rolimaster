# Monk

## Summary

Monks are semi spell users who combine the realm of [[Essence]] with martial training. Their base lists focus on personal enhancement, unusual movement, heightened senses, recovery, and disciplined control of the body.

## Classification

- Spell user: Semi
- Realms: [[Essence]] and Arms
- Prime stats: Self Discipline and Empathy

- Everyman Skills: Time Sense
- Occupational Skills: Meditation
- Restricted Skills: Channeling

## Spell Lists

- [[Body Reins]]: Allows the caster to perform unusual physical feats, such as breathing underwater.
- [[Body Renewal]]: Increases the natural healing rate of the caster's body.
- [[Evasions]]: Enables extraordinary evasive maneuvers.
- [[Mind Over Matter]]: Hardens the caster's body, improving the lethality of blows and defensive toughness.
- [[Monk's Bridge]]: Allows the caster to move in unusual ways.
- [[Monk's Sense]]: Enhances the caster's senses.
