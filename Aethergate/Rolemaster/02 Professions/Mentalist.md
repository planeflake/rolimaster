# Mentalist

## Summary

Mentalists are pure Mentalism spell users who specialize in the interaction of minds: perception, communication, control, attack, and mental merging.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Pure
- **Realm:** [[Mentalism]]
- **Prime stats:** Presence and Self Discipline
