# Healer

## Summary

Healers are pure Channeling spell users devoted to restoring damaged bodies and cleansing afflictions. Their base lists divide healing into blood, muscle, bone, organs, surface injuries, and purification.

## Spell Lists

- [[Blood Ways]]: Restores blood loss and blood-related injury.

## Mana World Notes

In [[Aethergate]], Healers are among the most rationed and protected Channeling specialists. Their work turns temple stores, vows, and sacred Font access into visible mercy: stopped bleeding, mended bones, clean wounds, restored organs, and survival after disasters.

Healers are also political pressure points. When mana runs thin, their waiting rooms reveal who has influence, who has coin, who has a patron, and who is being quietly left outside the door.
