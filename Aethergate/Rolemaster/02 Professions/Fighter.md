# Fighter

## Summary

Fighters are non-spell users trained to develop weapon skills and wear heavy armor efficiently. They are less suited to maneuvering, mechanical tasks, and magic.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** None
- **Realm:** None
- **Prime stats:** Constitution and Strength

## Profession Bonuses

| Area | Bonus |
|---|---:|
| Armor Group | +10 |
| Body Development | +10 |
| Combat Maneuvers | +10 |
| Weapon Group | +20 |

## Skill Notes

- **Everyman skills:** Situational Awareness - Combat, Leadership, Frenzy, one non-restricted Combat Maneuver, Boxing, Tackling
- **Occupational skills:** None
- **Restricted skills:** None

## Spell Development

The Fighter has no base lists. Open-list costs range from `25` for levels 1-5 to `125` for levels 21+, while closed-list costs range from `40` to `200`.

## Training Packages

See [[Training Package Index]]. Typical packages in the sourcebook are [[City Guard]], [[Knight]], and [[Soldier]].

## Source Reference

The complete skill-category development-point table is on sourcebook page 92.

## Typical Abilities
- Reliable weapon attacks, heavy armor, endurance, and direct battlefield control.

## Situation Examples
- **Street ambush:** Holds the narrowest approach and protects less durable companions.
- **Guarded doorway:** Forces or defends the entry with armor, strength, and weapon skill.
- **Tense negotiation:** Watches for physical danger while a social companion leads.
