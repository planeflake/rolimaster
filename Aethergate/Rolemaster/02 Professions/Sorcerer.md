# Sorcerer

## Summary

Sorcerers are hybrid spell users who combine Essence and Channeling. Their base magic focuses on the destruction of animate and inanimate material.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Hybrid
- **Realms:** [[Essence]] and [[Channeling]]
- **Prime stats:** Empathy, Intuition, and Self Discipline

## Skill Notes

- **Everyman skills:** Time Sense, Magic Ritual, Spell Mastery, Meditation, Channeling, Divination
- **Occupational skills:** None
- **Restricted skills:** None

## Spell Lists

- [[Flesh Destruction]]: Damages parts of the body.
- [[Fluid Destruction]]: Destroys and manipulates liquids.
- [[Gas Destruction]]: Destroys and manipulates gases.
- [[Mind Destruction]]: Damages mental processes.
- [[Solid Destruction]]: Destroys and manipulates solids.
- [[Soul Destruction]]: Damages aspects of the soul.

## Spell Development

Base lists cost `3/3/3`. Open-list costs progress from `4/4/4` to `6/6/6`, `8/8`, and `12`. Closed-list costs progress from `4/4/4` to `6/6/6`, `8/8`, `10/10`, and `25`.

The first five lists developed in a level use normal cost. Lists 6-10 cost `x2`, and later lists cost `x4`.
