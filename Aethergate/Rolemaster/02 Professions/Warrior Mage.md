# Warrior Mage

## Summary

Warrior Mages are semi spell users who combine the realm of Arms with [[Essence]]. Their base spells adapt Essence casting to combat and survival in hostile environments. They are elite fighters specialized in attacking enemy spell casters on their own terms.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Semi
- **Realms:** Arms and [[Essence]]
- **Prime stats:** Empathy and Constitution

## Spell Lists

- [[Combat Enhancement]]: Expands the Warrior Mage's melee-combat options.
- [[Combat Misdirections]]: Causes confusion among enemies.
- [[Detections and Protections]]: Provides an edge against more powerful spell casters.
- [[Warrior's Blade]]: Dedicates a weapon to the Warrior Mage and allows special spells to be cast upon it.
- [[Warrior's Enhancements]]: Supports combat in hostile environments.
- [[Will of the Warrior]]: Drives the Warrior Mage beyond normal endurance.
