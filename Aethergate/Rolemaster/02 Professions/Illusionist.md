# Illusionist

## Summary

Illusionists are pure spell users who specialize in illusion and misdirection. Their base magic manipulates sensory experience, and they have advantages with perception, stalking, and hiding.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Pure
- **Realm:** [[Essence]]
- **Prime stats:** Empathy and Reasoning

## Spell Development

Base lists cost `3/3/3`. Open and closed Essence lists cost `4/4/4` through level 20. For level 21+ lists, open costs rise to `6/6/6` and closed costs to `8/8`.

The first five lists developed in a level use normal cost. Lists 6-10 cost `x2`, and later lists cost `x4`.
