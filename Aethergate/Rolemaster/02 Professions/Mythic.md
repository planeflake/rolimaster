# Mythic

## Summary

Mythics are semi spell users who combine Arms with [[Channeling]]. They often stand outside normal church hierarchies and undertake secret missions for their religion and deity.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Semi
- **Realm:** [[Channeling]]
- **Prime stats:** Agility and Intuition

## Skill Notes

- **Everyman skills:** None
- **Occupational skills:** Religion
- **Restricted skills:** None

## Channeling Companion Spell Lists

- Holy Mission: Contacts the caster's religion and deity and supports missions.
- The Defender: Increases the caster's protections.
- The Eye: Gathers information and spies on targets.
- The Mask: Keeps the caster's actions secret and helps them blend into other faiths.
- The Will: Heals and enhances the caster.
- Vengeance: Hunts and combats specific enemies.
