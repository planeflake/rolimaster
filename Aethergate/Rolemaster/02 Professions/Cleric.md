# Cleric

## Summary

Clerics are pure Channeling spell users whose power is received through a deity. Their access to powerful magic is balanced by religious obligations.

## Mana World Notes

In [[Aethergate]], Clerics are public stewards of divine Channeling and social trust. They turn the power of temples, oaths, relics, and congregations into healing, protection, repulsion, and communal guidance.

Clerics are especially important during unstable Font cycles, when citizens need more than raw power: they need someone to decide who receives aid, which shrine stores are opened, and which spiritual obligations must be honored before magic is spent.

## Situation Examples
- **Undead incursion:** Repels the threat and protects companions from supernatural harm.
- **Wounded villagers:** Organizes aid and applies restorative magic.
- **Temple dispute:** Uses religious standing and communal knowledge to mediate.
