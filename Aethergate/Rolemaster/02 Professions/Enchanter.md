# Enchanter

## Summary

Enchanters are hybrid spell users who combine Essence and Mentalism. Their magic misdirects and manipulates the minds of humanoids, beasts, animals, and other sentient creatures through mental illusions, mind control, and memory alteration.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Hybrid
- **Realms:** [[Essence]] and [[Mentalism]]
- **Prime stats:** Self Discipline, Empathy, and Presence

- **Everyman skills:** Meditation, Time Sense, Lie Perception, Spell Mastery, Hypnosis
- **Occupational skills:** None
- **Restricted skills:** None

## Spell Lists

- [[Seeming Defenses]]: Conceals targets from the senses of others.
- [[Seeming Enhancement]]: Adds senses and enables seemings to affect non-humanoids.
- [[Seeming Law]]: Creates basic mental illusions.
- [[Seeming Mastery]]: Supports mind control and emotional manipulation.
- [[Seeming Memories]]: Enables mind probes, memory manipulation, and ability sharing.
- [[Seeming Projection]]: Causes sensory deprivation and illusory injuries.
