# Magician

## Summary

Magicians are pure Essence spell users whose base lists focus on elemental manipulation.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Pure
- **Realm:** [[Essence]]
- **Prime stats:** Empathy and Reasoning

## Spell Development

Base lists cost `3/3/3`. Open and closed lists cost `4/4/4` through level 20, with higher costs for level 21+ lists. Lists after the first five developed in a level receive increasing multipliers.

- **Burning building:** Controls fire or water to open a route for civilians.
- **Battlefield chokepoint:** Uses elemental force to deny an approach.
- **Hostile creature:** Chooses the element most likely to exploit its weakness.
