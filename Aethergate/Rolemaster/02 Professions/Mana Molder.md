# Mana Molder

## Summary

Mana Molders are pure spell users of Essence who concentrate on Mana Forms: solid manifestations of Essence. Their spells create temporary items, protective fields, and mana creatures ranging from simple servants to warriors capable of following basic orders.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Pure
- **Realm:** [[Essence]]
- **Prime stats:** Empathy and Agility

- **Everyman skills:** Time Sense, Spell Mastery, Meditation
- **Occupational skills:** None

## Spell Lists

- [[Mana Fields]]: Forms protective fields from Essence.
- [[Mana Items]]: Creates useful temporary items.
- [[Mana Servants]]: Creates magical servants for specific tasks.
- [[Mana Warriors]]: Creates and controls temporary warriors fashioned from Essence.
- [[Glyphs]]: Imbeds spells on air or liquids.
- [[Matter Molding]]: Shapes gases, liquids, and solids into different forms.
