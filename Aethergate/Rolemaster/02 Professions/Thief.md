# Thief

## Summary

Thieves are non-spell users who excel at mechanical manipulation, maneuvering, and stealth. They also develop weapon skills at a reasonable cost.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** None
- **Realm:** None
- **Prime stats:** Agility and Quickness

## Profession Bonuses

| Area | Bonus |
|---|---:|
| Athletic - Gymnastic | +5 |
| Awareness Group | +10 |
| Body Development | +5 |
| Self Control | +5 |
| Subterfuge Group | +15 |
| Weapon Group | +10 |

## Skill Notes

- **Everyman skills:** Choice of one Situational Awareness skill, Duping, Operating Equipment
- **Occupational skills:** Lock Lore
- **Restricted skills:** None

## Spell Development

The Thief has no base lists. Open-list costs range from `18` for levels 1-5 to `90` for levels 21+, while closed-list costs range from `35` to `175`.

## Training Packages

See [[Training Package Index]]. Typical packages in the sourcebook are [[Burglar]], [[City Guard]], and [[Scout]].

## Source Reference

The complete skill-category development-point table is on sourcebook page 93.

## Typical Abilities
- Stealth, locks, traps, observation, mobility, and opportunistic attacks.

## Situation Examples
- **Sealed archive:** Checks for traps, opens the lock, and searches hidden compartments.
- **Rooftop pursuit:** Uses climbing, balance, and route knowledge to intercept a target.
- **Tavern investigation:** Listens for rumors and notices who is watching the group.
