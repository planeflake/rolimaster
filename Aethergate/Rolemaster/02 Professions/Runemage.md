# Runemage

## Summary

Runemages are hybrid spell users of Essence and Channeling who concentrate on the power written and spoken language has over reality. Most are Nomenist casters. They use words, symbols, and runes to create enchantments such as wards while using the Primal Tongue to manipulate the physical world and its creatures.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Hybrid
- **Realms:** [[Essence]] and [[Channeling]]
- **Prime stats:** Empathy and Intuition

## Spell Lists

- [[Commanding Will]]: Bends a target to the caster's will, with greater power when a true name is known.
- [[Inscriptions]]: Imbeds spells into temporary signs or runes.
- [[Nomenclature Mastery]]: Discovers languages and the true names of people and items.
- [[Physical Manipulations]]: Manipulates the physical world, enhanced when a true name is known.
- [[Symbolism]]: Creates symbols of power that can be imbedded with other spells.
- [[Warding Mastery]]: Creates ward signs of power that protect small areas.
