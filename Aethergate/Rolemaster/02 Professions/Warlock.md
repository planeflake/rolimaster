# Warlock

## Summary

Warlocks are hybrid spell users who combine [[Channeling]] and [[Mentalism]]. They manipulate people and fate through harmful magic, broken faith, fear, revenge, transformation, and ill fortune.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Hybrid
- **Realms:** [[Channeling]] and [[Mentalism]]
- **Prime stats:** Intuition, Presence, and Self Discipline

## Skill Notes

- **Everyman skills:** Time Sense, Meditation, Channeling, Divination, Religion
- **Occupational skills:** None
- **Restricted skills:** None

## Channeling Companion Spell Lists

- Doom's Law: Brings death to the caster's enemies.
- Faith Destruction: Destroys a target's faith in deities and others.
- Look of Terror: Uses presence and reputation to command and frighten others.
- Revenging Law: Exacts revenge on those who have wronged the caster.
- Transformations: Changes the caster's body in useful and deadly ways.
- Wyrd Mastery: Manipulates fate and luck.
