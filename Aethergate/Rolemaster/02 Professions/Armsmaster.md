# Armsmaster

## Summary

Armsmasters are semi spell users of Mentalism. They serve as household knights, mercenary commanders, military officers, or elite specialists. Their magic improves personal martial ability, survival, military organization, and battlefield command.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Semi
- **Realm:** [[Mentalism]]
- **Prime stats:** Agility and Presence

- **Everyman skills:** Tactics, Siege Engineering

## Spell Lists

- [[Armor Mastery]]: Temporarily enhances the caster's personal armor and shield.

- [[Fortress Law]]: Provides protections for camps and strongholds.
- [[Martial Law]]: Supports personal and troop movement.
