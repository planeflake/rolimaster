# Animist

## Summary

Animists are pure Channeling spell users whose magic is bound to the living world. Their base lists focus on movement through natural terrain, plant and animal influence, herb lore, protection from the wild, and reading the signs of nature.

## Mana World Notes

In [[Aethergate]], Animists are the people who remember that mana is not only drawn through temples, towers, and conduits. They read the city's garden shrines, canal moss, courier birds, root-cracked ruins, and weather-touched stones as living instruments of the [[The Nine Fonts|Nine Fonts]].

Animists are especially useful when a district's artificial mana systems fail. They can identify which natural things are still carrying power, which herbs have absorbed a dangerous resonance, and which animals or plants are reacting to a coming Font shift.
