## Mana World Notes

In [[Aethergate]], Lay Healers often work where temples cannot reach or do not choose to spend power. They are prized in guild clinics, back-room surgeries, dueling schools, caravan houses, and quiet recovery rooms where injury must be handled without public religious attention.

Their Mentalism makes them especially valuable for trauma care, long recovery, nerve damage, and prosthetic adaptation. A good Lay Healer may know a patient's pain, fear, and physical limits with uncomfortable precision.
