# Summoner

## Summary

Summoners are hybrid spell users who combine [[Channeling]] and [[Essence]]. Their magic communicates with, summons, controls, and enhances creatures ranging from animals to extraplanar beings and distant allies.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Hybrid
- **Realms:** [[Channeling]] and [[Essence]]
- **Prime stats:** Empathy, Intuition, and Self Discipline

## Skill Notes

- **Everyman skills:** Time Sense, Magic Ritual, Spell Mastery, Meditation, Channeling, Circle Lore
- **Occupational skills:** None
- **Restricted skills:** None

## Channeling Companion Spell Lists

- Animal Summons: Summons animals to help the caster.
- Planar Summons: Summons angels, demons, and other extraplanar creatures.
- Summoning Bond: Enhances the caster's summoned creatures.
- Summoning Circles: Summons and controls creatures and imbeds spells.
- Summoning Mastery: Enhances summoning spells and abilities.
- Teleportals: Communicates with and summons distant people and creatures.
