# Ranger

## Summary

Rangers combine Channeling with arms training. Their base magic supports wilderness travel, weather, concealment, and survival.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Semi
- **Realm:** [[Channeling]]
- **Prime stats:** Constitution and Intuition
- **Special note:** `+10` to Stalking and Hiding, optionally limited to outdoor use by the GM
