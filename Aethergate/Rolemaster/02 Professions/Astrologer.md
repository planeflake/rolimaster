# Astrologer

## Summary

Astrologers are hybrid spell users who combine Channeling and Mentalism. Their magic gathers information by observing celestial bodies and supports detection, communion, precognition, and communication. Many abilities are fully usable only under starlight.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Hybrid
- **Realms:** [[Channeling]] and [[Mentalism]]
- **Prime stats:** Self Discipline, Intuition, and Presence

## Skill Notes

- **Everyman skills:** Basic Math, Channeling, Direction Sense, Divination, Divination Lore, Meditation, Time Sense
- **Occupational skills:** Astronomy, Stargazing, Weather Watching
- **Restricted skills:** None

## Mentalism Companion Spell Lists

- [[Far Voice]]: Supports mental communication and voice projection.
- [[Holy Vision]]: Provides knowledge through communing with deities and dreams.
- [[Starlights]]: Manipulates light and creates light-based attacks.
- [[Starlore]]: Assists with casting and interpreting horoscopes.
- [[Starsense]]: Enables mental detection and modifies the caster's senses.
- [[Way of the Voice]]: Supports communication, influence, and control.

## Core Vault Spell Lists

The core rules also associate Astrologers with [[Time's Bridge]]. It is retained as a core-rule alternative.
