# Seer

## Summary

Seers are pure Mentalism spell users who specialize in gathering information. Their magic evokes the past, scries the present, divines the future, and modifies the senses of themselves and others.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Pure
- **Realm:** [[Mentalism]]
- **Prime stats:** Self Discipline and Presence

## Skill Notes

- **Everyman skills:** Artifact Lore, History, Divination Lore, Lie Perception, Stargazing, Time Sense
- **Occupational skills:** Divination, Meditation
- **Restricted skills:** Channeling

## Mentalism Companion Spell Lists

- [[Far Visions]]: Modifies the caster's own senses.
- [[Future Visions]]: Provides precognition and divination of the future.
- [[Mind Visions]]: Supports mental detection, lie detection, and mind probes.
- [[Past Visions]]: Evokes the past of people, places, and objects as visions.
- [[Vision Borrowing]]: Enables scrying and the sharing of others' senses.
- [[Vision Guard]]: Provides defenses against evocation, scrying, and divination.

## Core Vault Spell Lists

The core rules also associate Seers with [[True Sight]], [[True Perception]], and [[Sense Through Others]]. These are retained as core-rule alternatives.

- **Unsolved murder:** Searches the past for details missed by ordinary investigation.
- **Dangerous route:** Looks ahead for likely trouble before the group commits.
- **Hidden chamber:** Borrows or extends perception to inspect what cannot be approached safely.
