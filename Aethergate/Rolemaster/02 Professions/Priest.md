# Priest

## Summary

Priests are configurable pure [[Channeling]] spell users designed for a specific religion. The companion provides a common template, then modifies it according to the deity's sphere of influence. This allows priesthoods from different faiths to have distinct skills, bonuses, spell lists, and special abilities.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Pure
- **Realm:** [[Channeling]]
- **Prime stats:** Intuition and Memory by default

## Priest Template

All priests begin with the common pure-Channeling template on source page 26. The selected sphere of influence then determines:

- Profession bonuses
- Skill classifications
- Base spell lists
- Special abilities
- An optional replacement for Memory as a prime stat

If a deity has multiple spheres, the GM may combine them using the averaging and skill-classification guidance in the source.

## Profession Bonuses

Determined by the deity's sphere of influence.

## Skill Notes

- **Everyman skills:** Determined by the deity's sphere of influence.
- **Occupational skills:** Determined by the deity's sphere of influence.
- **Restricted skills:** Determined by the deity's sphere of influence.

## Spell Lists

All spell lists granted to a Priest are base lists. The sphere of influence determines the normal lists for that religion, and the GM may adjust the selections to fit the deity.
