# Dabbler

## Summary

The Dabbler is a semi-spell user combining Essence with arms training. Their magic focuses on stealth, detection, perception, movement, locks, and traps.

## Classification

- **Spell use:** Semi
- **Realm:** [[Essence]]
- **Prime stats:** Agility and Empathy

- **Guarded reception:** Blends into the crowd and quietly gathers information.
