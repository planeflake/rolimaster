# Spell Triggers

## Summary

Open Arcane spell-storage trigger magic for detecting, analyzing, storing, sensing, delaying, bypassing, and enhancing trigger conditions.

## Realm

- [[Arcane]]

## Profession / Category

- [[Open Arcane]]
