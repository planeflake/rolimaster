## List Notes

The list helps the caster overcome locks, bindings, barriers, and distance. The source includes special lock-difficulty modifiers and familiarity-based risk for the highest teleportation effect.

## Spell Descriptions

| Level | Spell | Description |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Locklore | Analyzes one lock and grants the caster `+20` to pick it. Anyone receiving the analysis receives `+10`. |
| 2 | Unlock I | Has a `30%` base chance to open a non-magical lock, with a `10%` chance of setting off associated traps if it fails. |
| 3 | Ungag | Loosens a minor organic covering on the caster's head, including a gag or blindfold. |
| 4 | Leave Item I | Teleports one inanimate object up to `100'` away without passing through barriers; weight is limited to `1 lb` per caster level. |
| 5 | Unlock II | As Unlock I, but with a `50%` chance to open the lock and an `8%` trap risk. |
| 6 | Untie | Unties ropes or similar bindings restraining the caster. |
| 7 | Minor Long Door | Teleports the caster up to `50'`, specifying the destination distance and direction. Arrival inside solid or liquid material prevents movement and stuns the caster. |
| 8 | Unlock III | As Unlock I, but with a `70%` chance to open the lock and a `6%` trap risk. |
| 9 | Unbind | Gives a `75%` base chance to open each lock or shackle binding the caster. |
| 10 | Leaving I | Teleports the caster up to `100'` to a reachable destination with no intervening barriers. |
| 11 | Passing | Allows the caster to pass through inanimate material at `5'` per minute. |
| 12 | Item Long Door I | As Leave Item I, but may move an object through barriers by specifying destination distance and direction. |
| 13 | Unlock IV | As Unlock I, but with a `90%` chance to open the lock and a `4%` trap risk. |
| 14 | Long Door I | As Minor Long Door, but moves the caster up to `100'`. |
| 15 | Unbind True | As Unbind, but also affects fixed shackles such as riveted restraints. |
| 16 | Leaving II | As Leaving I, but moves the caster up to `200'`. |
| 18 | Flattening | Flattens the caster into two dimensions, allowing movement through cracks. |
| 19 | Long Door II | As Minor Long Door, but moves the caster up to `200'`. |
| 20 | Passing True | As Passing, but movement through material increases to `10'` per minute. |
| 25 | Portal | Opens a temporary `3' x 6' x 3'` portal through a solid surface. |
| 30 | Unlock True | As Unlock III, but affects all locks in the area of effect. |
| 50 | Teleport I | As Minor Long Door, but moves the caster up to `10 miles` per level. Destination familiarity determines the risk of error. |
