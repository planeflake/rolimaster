## List Notes

The list alters how targets perceive identities and situations through false credentials, invisibility, cloaking, misleading impressions, eyewitness manipulation, and displacement.

## Spell Descriptions

| Level | Spell | Description |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | False Credentials | A target believes the caster has suitable credentials for the current situation, such as passwords or documents. |
| 2 | Unseen I | Makes one object invisible for up to 24 hours unless it is struck violently or makes a violent move such as an attack. |
| 3 | Eyewitness III | Up to three targets incorrectly identify the caster as someone else, all describing the same false identity. |
| 4 | Cloaking III | Makes the caster invisible to three targets. Obvious actions can allow further resistance rolls; attacking permits a resistance roll with a `+50` modifier. |
| 5 | Misleading I | Skews one target's perception, applying `-10` to Awareness - Searching maneuvers and orientation rolls. |
| 6 | Unseen II | As Unseen I, but affects two objects. |
| 7 | Cloaking V | As Cloaking III, but affects five targets. |
| 8 | Eyewitness V | As Eyewitness III, but affects five targets. |
| 9 | Cloaking Sphere I | As Cloaking III, but affects targets within a `10'` radius. Each target resists once. |
| 10 | Misleading III | As Misleading I, but the penalties are `-30`. |
| 11 | Cloaking X | As Cloaking III, but affects ten targets. |
| 12 | Displacement I | Offsets the caster's apparent location, causing attacks to miss automatically `10%` of the time. The miss chance falls by `5%` after each miss. |
| 13 | Eyewitness X | As Eyewitness III, but affects ten targets. |
| 14 | Cloaking Sphere II | As Cloaking Sphere I, but affects a `20'` radius. |
| 15 | Misleading V | As Misleading I, but the penalties are `-50`. |
| 16 | Cloaking XX | As Cloaking III, but affects twenty targets. |
| 18 | Mass Eyewitness | As Eyewitness III, but affects one target per caster level. |
| 19 | Cloaking Sphere III | As Cloaking Sphere I, but affects a `30'` radius. |
| 20 | Misleading True | As Misleading I, but the penalties are `-100`. |
| 25 | Displacement II | As Displacement I, but the initial automatic-miss chance is `20%`. |
| 30 | True Cloaking | As Cloaking I, but affects up to one target per caster level within the caster's field of vision. |
| 50 | Eyewitness True | As Eyewitness III, but affects everyone who sees the caster. |

## Special Notes

- Many effects on this list alter a target's perception of an event. When the duration expires, the target remembers the event according to that altered perception.
- The source points to its general invisibility rules for detection and interaction details.
- If the caster has a studied patsy from [[Assassination Mastery]], Eyewitness spells may make targets perceive the caster as that patsy.
