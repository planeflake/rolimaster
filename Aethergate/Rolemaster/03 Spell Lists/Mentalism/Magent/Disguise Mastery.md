## List Notes

The list combines glamours, physical disguises, false magical impressions, and identity-obscuring effects. Several higher-level spells build on a studied person's appearance or identity.

## Spell Descriptions

| Level | Spell | Description |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Study Disguise | Studies one person for later disguise spells. The caster may retain as many studied disguises as levels. |
| 2 | Light Glamour | Changes the visual appearance of one object within the spell's size limit. |
| 3 | Facade I | Creates a limited visual illusion around the caster, allowing an appearance within `20%` of the caster's size. |
| 4 | Shadow Assassin | Shadows the caster's deeds against divination, revealing only vague impressions without identifying details. |
| 5 | Misfeel Kind | Makes magical or mental detections perceive the caster as a chosen race. |
| 6 | Facade II | As Facade I, but includes one additional sense. |
| 7 | Disguise I | Physically changes the caster into the form and visual appearance of a studied person. |
| 8 | Unknown Assassin | As Shadow Assassin, but reveals no identifying information about the caster. |
| 9 | Facade III | As Facade I, but includes two additional senses. |
| 10 | Disguise II | As Disguise I, but also reproduces the studied person's vocal characteristics. |
| 11 | Misfeel Calling | As Misfeel Kind, but magical or mental detections perceive a chosen profession. |
| 12 | Mind Tongue | Allows mental communication with one target in the manner that would normally be used verbally. |
| 13 | Disguise III | As Disguise II, but also reproduces obvious mannerisms such as handedness or a limp. |
| 14 | Facade V | As Facade I, but includes all senses. |
| 15 | Misfeel Power | As Misfeel Kind, but the caster appears to be a chosen level from first level to twice the caster's actual level. |
| 16 | Disguise IV | As Disguise III, but also reproduces subtle mannerisms, making detection difficult except for close associates. |
| 17 | Unpresence | As Misfeel Kind, but presence-detection spells perceive the caster as having no presence. |
| 18 | False Assassin | As Shadow Assassin, but the caster's deeds appear to have been performed by the currently studied patsy. |
| 19 | Disguise V | As Disguise IV, but includes even very subtle mannerisms. |
| 20 | Misfeel | Applies all of the lower Misfeel effects at the same time. |
| 25 | Nondetect | Prevents detection spells from detecting the caster or objects carried by the caster. |
| 30 | Misfeel True | As Misfeel, but concentration is no longer required. |
| 50 | Disguise Mastery | Allows the caster to use one lower-level spell from this list each round. |
