## List Notes

The list covers poison knowledge, resistance, preparation, analysis, neutralization, transfer, and supernatural delivery. The source notes that effects requiring the caster to touch a poison also grant temporary immunity to that poison.

## Spell Descriptions

| Level | Spell | Description |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Poison Lore | Identifies the type of poison needed for a particular effect and may grant `+25` to Poison Lore when the GM deems it appropriate. |
| 2 | Poison Resistance I | Grants an additional resistance roll against poisons for the duration. |
| 3 | Apply Poison I | Causes one dose of poison to adhere to an inanimate solid surface and wait for a target. |
| 4 | Work Poison V | Prepares an existing poison up to fifth-level potency for use after `100` minutes of concentration. |
| 5 | Poison Analysis | Analyzes a poison to determine its exact effects and necessary antidotes. |
| 6 | Delay Poison | Temporarily suppresses all effects from one poison in the caster; the effects resume after the duration unless delayed again. |
| 7 | Transfer Poison I | Transfers one poison dose from a touched target into the caster, who can then delay or neutralize it. |
| 8 | Apply Poison II | As Apply Poison I, but affects two doses, which may be applied to different surfaces. |
| 9 | Neutralize Poison | Gives one poison dose within the caster a `50%` chance to be neutralized, modified by potency, and suppresses its effects for the duration regardless. |
| 10 | Work Poison X | As Work Poison V, but prepares poisons up to tenth-level potency. |
| 11 | Venom | Converts a touched poison into venom so it becomes effective when introduced into a target's bloodstream. |
| 12 | Apply Poison III | As Apply Poison II, but affects up to three doses. |
| 13 | Neutralize Poison True | As Neutralize Poison, but the base neutralization chance is `100%`, modified by potency. |
| 14 | Transfer Poison II | Transfers one poison dose from a touched target to another touched target. |
| 15 | Venom's Touch | Coats a target's hands with poison without harming that target. |
| 16 | Work Poison True | As Work Poison V, but prepares a poison of any potency. |
| 17 | Apply Poison IV | As Apply Poison II, but affects up to four doses. |
| 18 | Contact Poison | As Venom, but converts the poison into a contact poison affecting skin contact. |
| 19 | Transfer Poison III | Transfers one poison dose from a touched target to any target within range. |
| 20 | Venom's Breath | Allows the caster to ingest one dose of poison without harm, then deliver it to a nearby target by kiss, whisper, spit, or a similar mouth-based action. |
| 25 | Venom's Glare | As Venom's Breath, but delivers the poison to a single target within range through an obvious hostile glare. |
| 30 | Venom's Cloud | Disperses poison into a mist with a radius of `1'` per dose; the poison lingers normally until the spell ends. |
| 50 | Venom's Sight | As Venom's Glare, but with increased range. |

## Special Notes

Any spell on this list that requires the caster to touch a poison grants the caster immunity to that poison for the duration of the spell.
