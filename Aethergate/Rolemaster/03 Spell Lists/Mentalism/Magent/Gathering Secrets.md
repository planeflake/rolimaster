## List Notes

The list gathers information through copying, duplication, remote sensory observation, material sight, and evidence-location effects.

## Spell Descriptions

| Level | Spell | Description |
|---:|---|---|
| 1 | Copy I | Copies a document while the caster concentrates, transferring visible content onto a blank page at one page or scene per minute. |
| 2 | Listen | Allows the caster to hear from a fixed point up to `10'` away without intervening barriers. |
| 3 | Duplicate I | Creates a hardened-clay copy of an object up to `6" x 6" x 6"` after one minute of concentration. |
| 4 | Sensory Marker | Marks one location for later use with Far Sense spells. The caster may maintain as many markers as levels. |
| 5 | Copy II | As Copy I, but copies one page or scene every four rounds. |
| 6 | Far Sense I | Uses one of the caster's senses from a marked location within range while the caster remains immobile and concentrates. |
| 7 | Locate Evidence I | Indicates direction, distance, and appearance of one piece of evidence at the scene that could implicate the studied patsy. |
| 8 | Watch | As Listen, but provides sight from the fixed point and allows rotation. |
| 9 | Duplicate II | As Duplicate I, but duplicates objects up to `3' x 3' x 3'`. |
| 10 | Copy III | As Copy I, but copies one page or scene every two rounds. |
| 11 | Far Sense II | As Far Sense I, but uses up to two senses at the marked location. |
| 12 | Woodsight | Allows the caster to see through wood, up to `1"` per level. |
| 13 | Locate Evidence II | As Locate Evidence I, but locates two pieces of evidence and makes the evidence incriminating rather than merely circumstantial. |
| 14 | Duplicate III | As Duplicate I, but duplicates objects up to `6' x 6' x 6'`. |
| 15 | Copy IV | As Copy I, but copies one page or scene per round. |
| 16 | Greater Far Sense I | As Far Sense I, but the caster may move while maintaining concentration. |
| 17 | Stonesight | As Woodsight, but sees through stone. |
| 18 | Locate Evidence III | As Locate Evidence I, but locates three pieces of evidence that will prove the studied patsy's guilt. |
| 19 | Greater Far Sense II | As Far Sense II, but the caster may move while maintaining concentration. |
| 20 | Ironsight | As Woodsight, but sees through iron or steel. |
| 25 | Long Far Sense I | As Far Sense I, but with increased range. |
| 30 | Copy V | As Copy I, but copies two pages or scenes per round. |
| 50 | Copy True | Copies a complete bound volume onto an equal number of pages at five pages per round, including color images, without requiring the caster to remain with the material. |
