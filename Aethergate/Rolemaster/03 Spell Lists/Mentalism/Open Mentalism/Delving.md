# Delving

## Summary

Open Mentalism information spells for reading items, power, origins, curses, stonework, and construction.

## Realm

- [[Mentalism]]

## Profession / Category

- [[Open Mentalism]]

## Spell Grid

| Level | Spell | Area of Effect | Duration | Range | Type | Description |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Item Feel | 1 object | - | touch | I | Gives the caster a basic idea of an item's purpose, if any. |
| 2 | Detect Power | varies | `1 min/lvl(C)` | touch | I | Detects power in an object, but not its realm or amount; the caster may concentrate on a different object each round. |
| 3 | Origins | 1 object | - | touch | I | Gives a general idea of an item's place of origin. |
| 4 | No spell listed | - | - | - | - | No spell is listed at this level in the source. |
| 5 | Detect Curse | 1 object | - | touch | I | Detects whether a curse is on an item. |
| 6 | Power Lore | 1 object | - | touch | I | Gives the origins of an item's power. |
| 7 | Rock Lore | 1 rock | - | touch | I | Reveals where, when, and how the examined piece of rock was worked. |
| 8 | Item Vision | 1 object | varies | touch | I | Gives a vision of a significant event in the item's past. |
| 9 | No spell listed | - | - | - | - | No spell is listed at this level in the source. |
| 10 | Delving | 1 object | - | touch | I | Gives significant details about an item's construction and purpose, but not its specific powers. |

## Special Notes

- A curse is usually detrimental and magical; the GM may decide these spells apply only to magical curses.
- The source points to the general information-spell rules for more handling.
