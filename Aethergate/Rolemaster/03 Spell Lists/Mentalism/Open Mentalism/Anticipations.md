# Anticipations

## Summary

Open Mentalism spells for short-term foresight, intuition, hostile intent, dreams, and sensing recent events.

## Realm

- [[Mentalism]]

## Profession / Category

- [[Open Mentalism]]

## Spell Grid

| Level | Spell | Area of Effect | Duration | Range | Type | Description |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Anticipate Missile | 1 missile | - | self | I | Alerts the caster to missiles fired at them that round; if able to move, the caster gains `+50` DB against one chosen missile attack. |
| 2 | Anticipate Blow | 1 attack | - | self | I | As Anticipate Missile, but applies to melee attacks. |
| 3 | Guess | caster | - | self | I | Biases an uncertain choice among a limited set of options in the caster's favor by `25%`; the caster cannot reuse Guess for the same information until skill rank improves. |
| 4 | Anticipate Spell | 1 spell | - | self | I | As Anticipate Missile, but applies to spell attacks, giving defensive bonuses against directed elemental, area elemental, or base spell attacks. |
| 5 | Intuitions I | caster | - | self | I | Gives the caster a vision of what will probably happen in the next minute if they take a specified action. |
| 6 | Anticipate Hostility | `10'R` | - | self | I | Alerts the caster to active hostile intent directed specifically at them within the area. |
| 7 | No spell listed | - | - | - | - | No spell is listed at this level in the source. |
| 8 | Dream I | caster | sleep | self | I | Produces a dream about a topic chosen before sleeping; usable once per night and requires at least one normal sleep cycle. |
| 9 | Room Feel I | `1'R/lvl` | varies | `100'` | I | Shows what occurred in a room or place, limited to up to one minute per level into the past and constrained by the area of effect. |
| 10 | Anticipations | 1 being | - | `100'` | Pm | Predicts the target's most probable action in the next round in broad terms, allowing the caster to declare after hearing the target's stated intent. |

## Special Notes

- Guess requires finite, countable options.
- Dream interpretation should use symbolism.
- The source points to the general information-spell rules for additional handling.
