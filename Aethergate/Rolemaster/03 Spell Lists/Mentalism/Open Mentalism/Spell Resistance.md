# Spell Resistance

## Summary

Open Mentalism protective spells that resist elemental attacks, cancel incoming realm spells, and shield against Mentalism or Essence attacks.

## Realm

- [[Mentalism]]

## Profession / Category

- [[Open Mentalism]]

## Spell Grid

| Level | Spell | Area of Effect | Duration | Range | Type | Description |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Protection I | caster | `1 min/lvl` | self | U | Subtracts `5` from elemental attack rolls against the caster and adds `5` to the caster's resistance rolls versus spells. |
| 2 | Cancel Mentalism | 1 spell | C | self | F | Forces an incoming Mentalism spell targeting the caster to first make a resistance roll; if it fails, the incoming spell has no effect on the caster. |
| 3 | No spell listed | - | - | - | - | No spell is listed at this level in the source. |
| 4 | Cancel Essence | 1 spell | C | self | F | As Cancel Mentalism, but affects Essence spells. |
| 5 | Protection II | caster | `1 min/lvl` | self | U | As Protection I, but the bonuses and penalties are `10`. |
| 6 | Cancel Channeling | 1 spell | C | self | F | As Cancel Mentalism, but affects Channeling spells. |
| 7 | No spell listed | - | - | - | - | No spell is listed at this level in the source. |
| 8 | Mind Shield | caster | C | self | U | Creates a shield that subtracts `10` from frontal Mentalism attack rolls and can parry like a normal shield if the caster takes no other action that round. |
| 9 | No spell listed | - | - | - | - | No spell is listed at this level in the source. |
| 10 | Essence Shield | caster | C | self | U | As Mind Shield, but effective only against Essence spells. |
