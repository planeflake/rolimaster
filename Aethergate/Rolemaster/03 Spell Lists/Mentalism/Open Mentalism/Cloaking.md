# Cloaking

## Summary

Open Mentalism spells for blurring, shadowing, invisibility, cloaking, disguise, and mental shadow duplicates.

## Realm

- [[Mentalism]]

## Profession / Category

- [[Open Mentalism]]

## Spell Grid

| Level | Spell | Area of Effect | Duration | Range | Type | Description |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Blur | caster | `1 min/lvl` | self | U | Makes the caster appear blurred to attackers, subtracting `10` from attacks directed at them. |
| 2 | Shadow | caster | `10 min/lvl` | self | U | Makes the caster and carried objects appear shadowed, making them almost invisible in dark areas and often aiding Stalking/Hiding. |
| 3 | Unseen | object | `24 hours` | touch | U | Makes one object invisible until the duration ends, it is struck violently, or it makes a violent move. |
| 4 | Cloaking I | 1 target | `1 min/lvl(C)` | `10'/lvl` | Fm | On a failed resistance roll, one target cannot see the caster; obvious actions may allow another resistance roll and attacks allow a resistance roll with `+50`. |
| 5 | Facades I | caster | `1 hr/lvl` | self | E | A limited illusion that makes the caster look like any humanoid figure within `20%` of their size. |
| 6 | Cloaking III | 3 targets | `1 min/lvl(C)` | `10'/lvl` | Fm | As Cloaking I, but affects three targets with one base attack roll. |
| 7 | Cloaking Sphere I | `10'R` | C | self | Fm | As Cloaking I, but affects targets in the area; each target resists once, and a successful resistance ends that target's need to resist again. |
| 8 | Cloaking V | 5 targets | `1 min/lvl(C)` | `10'/lvl` | Fm | As Cloaking I, but affects five targets with one base attack roll. |
| 9 | Cloaking Sphere II | `20'R` | C | self | Fm | As Cloaking Sphere I, but affects a `20'` radius. |
| 10 | Shadow Mentalist I | varies | `1 min/lvl` | `100'` | F | Creates a duplicate of the caster that either mimics them or moves under concentration; while concentrating, the caster can merge and split from the duplicate to confuse observers. |

## Special Notes

- Cloaking spells are not invisibility spells; normal invisibility rules such as becoming visible after attacking do not apply.
