# Brilliance

## Summary

Open Mentalism light, darkness, aura, and shock spells.

- [[Mentalism]]

## Profession / Category

## Spell Grid

| Level | Spell | Area of Effect | Duration | Range | Type | Description |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Projected Light | `50' beam` | `10 min/lvl` | self | E | Projects a flashlight-like beam from the caster's palm; closing the hand shuts it off until opened again. |
| 2 | Glow | caster | `1 min/lvl` | self | E | Creates a small soft light, enough for Nightvision to work properly but not enough for easy movement in darkness. |
| 3 | Light I | `10'R` | `10 min/lvl` | touch | E | Lights a `10'` radius around the touched point; if the point moves, the light moves with it. |
| 4 | Self Aura | caster | `10 min/lvl` | self | U | Creates a bright aura around the caster, making them seem more powerful and subtracting `5` from all attacks against them. |
| 5 | Light Eruption | `10'R` | - | `100'` | F | Creates a burst of intense light; targets in the area are stunned for one round per `10` failure. |
| 6 | Shock Bolt | 1 target | - | `100'` | DE | Fires a bolt of intense charged light, resolved on the source's Bolt Spell Attack Table. |
| 7 | Darkness I | `10'R` | `10 min/lvl` | touch | E | As Light I, but creates a `10'` radius of normal darkness. |
| 8 | True Aura | caster | `10 min/lvl` | self | U | As Self Aura, but the aura appears extremely powerful and subtracts `15` from all attacks against the caster. |
| 9 | No spell listed | - | - | - | - | No spell is listed at this level in the source. |
| 10 | Light V | `50'R` | `10 min/lvl` | touch | E | As Light I, but creates a `50'` radius of light. |

## Special Notes

- Aura spells from this list are not cumulative with each other or with Blur.
