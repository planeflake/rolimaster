# Detections

## Summary

Open Mentalism perception spells for detecting realms, emotions, invisibility, evil, traps, power, and spell type.

## Realm

- [[Mentalism]]

## Profession / Category

- [[Open Mentalism]]

## Spell Grid

| Level | Spell | Area of Effect | Duration | Range | Type | Description |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Detect Mentalism | `5'R` | `1 min/lvl(C)` | `50'` | P | Detects active Mentalism spells or items from the Mentalism realm; the caster can concentrate on one `5'` radius area each round. |
| 2 | Detect Essence | `5'R` | `1 min/lvl(C)` | `50'` | P | As Detect Mentalism, but detects the Essence realm. |
| 3 | Detect Channeling | `5'R` | `1 min/lvl(C)` | `50'` | P | As Detect Mentalism, but detects the Channeling realm. |
| 4 | Detect Emotion | `5'R` | `1 min/lvl(C)` | `50'` | P | Detects strong emotions and their direction, but detects nothing if only normal feelings are present. |
| 5 | Detect Invisible | `5'R` | `1 min/lvl(C)` | `50'` | P | Detects invisible things; attacks against something detected this way are at `-50`. |
| 6 | Detect Evil | `5'R` | `1 min/lvl(C)` | `50'` | P | Detects evil beings or items created by evil or long used by a very evil person. |
| 7 | Detect Traps | `5'R` | `1 min/lvl(C)` | `50'` | P | Gives a `75%` chance to detect a trap, modified by trap sophistication. |
| 8 | Perceive Power I | `5'R` | `1 min/lvl(C)` | `50'` | P | Estimates the power or level of the examined person, item, or spell. |
| 9 | No spell listed | - | - | - | - | No spell is listed at this level in the source. |
| 10 | Power Typing | `5'R` | `1 min/lvl(C)` | `50'` | P | Analyzes a spell's general type and source list; for a person, gives the person's profession. |

## Special Notes

- For Detect Evil, evil is defined as something that causes or sources intentional suffering, injury, or destruction.
