# Channels

## Summary

Cleric spells for creating holy water, calming or stunning targets, inducing sleep, and transmitting Channeling spells to another spell user.

## Spell Details

### 1. Create Holy Water

The caster transforms up to four ounces of clean, clear water into holy water. Casting takes one hour. The holy water loses its sacred quality if it is not placed in a clean receptacle.

### 2. Calming

The caster channels raw power from their divine source toward one target. On a failed resistance roll, the target cannot initiate aggressive or offensive actions. The target may still fight in self-defense if attacked.

### 3. Channels I

The next 1st-level Channeling spell the caster begins casting on the following round is transmitted to another Channeling spell user instead of taking effect normally. The recipient becomes the effective caster and must use the spell immediately.

Normal restrictions for the transmitted spell still apply. The caster must be able to see the recipient or know their precise location. The recipient must know when the transmitted spell is arriving, although they do not need to know which spell it will be.

### 5. Stunning

The caster directs raw divine power at one target. On a failed resistance roll, the target is stunned for the indicated duration.

### 6. Channels III

As [[#3. Channels I|Channels I]], except a spell of up to 3rd level may be transmitted.

### 8. Golden Slumbers

The caster sends one target into deep sleep. The first minute is magical and the target cannot be awakened by ordinary means. After that minute, normal waking methods work, although the target may naturally sleep for a full eight hours.

### 10. Channels V

As [[#3. Channels I|Channels I]], except a spell of up to 5th level may be transmitted.

## Special Notes

The appearance and use of these spells may vary with the cleric's deity. A GM can substitute deity-appropriate effects where useful. For example, a fire deity might replace a stunning effect with an elemental fire attack.
