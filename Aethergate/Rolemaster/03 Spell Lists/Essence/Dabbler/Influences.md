# Influences

## Summary

Dabbler spells for sleep, charm, suggestion, telepathy, and temporarily holding a humanoid target.

## Realm

- [[Essence]]

## Profession / Category

- [[Dabbler]]

## Spell Grid

| Level | Spell | Area of Effect | Duration | Range | Type | Description |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Sleep III | varies | - | `100'` | Fm | Sends targets into natural sleep, affecting up to three total levels; the first two rounds are magical sleep and cannot be broken by normal waking methods. |
| 2 | Charm Animals | `1 animal/lvl` | `1 hour` | `100'` | Fm | Target animals believe the caster is a good friend. |
| 3 | Charm Kind | 1 target | `1 hr/lvl` | `100'` | Fm | A target humanoid believes the caster is a good friend. |
| 4 | Sleep V | varies | - | `100'` | Fm | As Sleep III, but affects up to five total levels. |
| 5 | Suggestion | 1 target | varies | `100'` | Fm | The target follows one suggested act, provided the act is not completely alien or self-destructive. |
| 6 | Telepathy I | 1 target | `1 rnd/lvl(C)` | `2'/lvl` | Fm | Lets the caster read surface thoughts; if the target's resistance roll succeeds by more than `25`, the target realizes someone tried to read their thoughts. |
| 7 | Sleep VII | varies | - | `100'` | Fm | As Sleep III, but affects up to seven total levels. |
| 8 | Hold Kind | 1 target | C | `100'` | Fm | Holds a target humanoid to `25%` activity. |
| 9 | Greater Charm Kind | `1 target/5 lvl` | `1 hr/lvl` | `100'` | Fm | As Charm Kind, but affects one target per five caster levels, or fraction thereof. |
| 10 | Sleep X | varies | - | `100'` | Fm | As Sleep III, but affects up to ten total levels. |
