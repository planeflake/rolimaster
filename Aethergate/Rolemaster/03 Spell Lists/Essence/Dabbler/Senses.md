# Senses

## Summary

Dabbler spells that enhance hearing, sight, smell, touch, peripheral vision, darkvision, watervision, and remote sensing.

## Realm

- [[Essence]]

## Profession / Category

- [[Dabbler]]

## Spell Grid

| Level | Spell | Area of Effect | Duration | Range | Type | Description |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Sly Ears | caster | `10 min/lvl` | self | U | Doubles normal hearing and grants bonuses to awareness skills involving hearing. |
| 2 | Nightvision | caster | `10 min/lvl` | self | U | Lets the caster see `100'` on a normal night as if it were daylight. |
| 3 | Scent | caster | `10 min/lvl` | self | U | As Sly Ears, but for smell, granting an extremely acute sense of smell and related awareness bonuses. |
| 4 | Sensory Marker | caster | P | touch | U | Marks one location so the caster can later locate it with a Far Sense spell; the caster can maintain up to one marker per level. |
| 5 | Far Sense I | caster | `1 min/lvl(C)` | `50'/lvl` | U | Lets the caster use one sense from a marked location within range; the remote point can be rotated, but the caster must remain immobile and concentrate. |
| 6 | Sidevision | caster | `10 min/lvl` | self | U | Gives the caster a `300 degree` field of vision and reduces flank and rear attack bonuses against them. |
| 7 | Touch | caster | `10 min/lvl` | self | U | Grants extreme tactile sensitivity and a bonus to actions depending on touch, such as picking locks or disarming traps. |
| 8 | Watervision | caster | `10 min/lvl` | self | U | Lets the caster see `100'` through water, including murky water, as if it were daylight. |
| 9 | Darkvision | caster | `10 min/lvl` | self | U | As Nightvision, but the caster can see through any darkness and needs no light. |
| 10 | Far Sense II | caster | `1 min/lvl(C)` | `50'/lvl` | U | As Far Sense I, but the caster may use up to two senses at the marked location. |
