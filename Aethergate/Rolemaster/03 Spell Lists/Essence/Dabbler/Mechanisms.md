# Mechanisms

## Summary

Dabbler spells for understanding, tuning, jamming, unlocking, operating, and disarming mechanical devices.

## Realm

- [[Essence]]

## Profession / Category

- [[Dabbler]]

## Spell Grid

| Level | Spell | Area of Effect | Duration | Range | Type | Description |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Machine Lore | 1 object | - | touch | I | Analyzes one machine, revealing its purpose and operation; grants the caster a special bonus to operate it and a smaller bonus to those they instruct. |
| 2 | Tune | 1 object | - | touch | U | Restores a machine's efficiency to its original level, such as returning a rusty lock to normal operation. |
| 3 | Jam I | 1 trap | - | touch | U | Has a `50%` chance to jam the target mechanism; if jammed, it has only a `5%` chance to operate each time it is used or triggered. |
| 4 | Unlock I | 1 lock | - | touch | U | Has a `30%` chance to open a nonmagical lock; a failed attempt has a chance to set off associated traps. |
| 5 | Untrap I | 1 trap | - | touch | U | As Unlock I, but for disarming traps; a failed attempt has a chance to trigger the trap. |
| 6 | Operate I | 1 machine | `1 min/lvl` | touch | U | As Unlock I, but operates a nonmagical machine in its normal capacity for the spell duration. |
| 7 | Detect Active Power | `5'R` | `1 min/lvl(C)` | self | P | Alerts the caster when active magic is within the area, though not its nature, realm, or exact source. |
| 8 | Jam II | 1 trap | - | touch | U | As Jam I, but with a `70%` chance to jam and only a `4%` chance to set off the mechanism. |
| 9 | Unlock II | 1 lock | - | touch | U | As Unlock I, but with a `50%` chance to open the lock and an `8%` chance to set off associated traps. |
| 10 | Untrap II | 1 trap | - | touch | U | As Untrap I, but with a `50%` chance to disarm the trap and an `8%` chance to trigger it. |

## Special Notes

- For this list, a machine is a man-made device built to perform a specific function.
- Percentage chances are modified by the sophistication of the trap, lock, or machine.
