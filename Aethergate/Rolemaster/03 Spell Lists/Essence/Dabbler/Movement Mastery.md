# Movement Mastery

## Summary

Dabbler spells for running, jumping, landing safely, silent movement, swimming, balance, climbing surfaces, diving, and leaving no tracks.

## Realm

- [[Essence]]

## Profession / Category

- [[Dabbler]]

## Spell Grid

| Level | Spell | Area of Effect | Duration | Range | Type | Description |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Run | caster | `10 min/lvl` | self | U | Lets the caster move at twice normal walking pace while expending exhaustion as if walking; the spell ends when the caster stops or takes another action. |
| 2 | Longjump | caster | - | self | U | Lets the caster jump much farther horizontally or vertically and safely land from jumps within the spell's limits. |
| 3 | Landing | caster | - | self | U | Lets the caster land safely from a fall up to `20'` per caster level, reducing longer falls by that amount. |
| 4 | Shadow Steps | caster | `1 min/lvl` | self | U | Silences the caster's movement while they move no faster than walking pace. |
| 5 | Underwater Movement | caster | `10 min/lvl` | self | U | Allows the caster to move underwater as if on land. |
| 6 | Balance I | caster | `1 rnd/lvl` | self | U | Grants `+50` to balance-based movement maneuvers performed at walking pace. |
| 7 | Spider's Step | caster | `1 min/lvl` | self | U | Allows half-walking movement along solid surfaces angled up to `90 degrees`, while maintaining three-point contact. |
| 8 | Long Dive | caster | - | self | U | Lets the caster dive safely through the air up to `50'` per level if sufficient water is present. |
| 9 | Traceless Passing | caster | C | self | U | Allows walking-pace movement without leaving tracks or other visible signs of passage. |
| 10 | Spider's Step True | caster | `1 min/lvl` | self | U | As Spider's Step, but allows movement along any surface, including ceilings. |

## Special Notes

- The starred source entries refer to the source's movement-spell encumbrance limits.
