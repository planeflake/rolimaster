# Spell Wall

## Summary

Open Essence defensive spells for spell resistance, cancellation, dispelling, and realm-specific shields.

## Realm

- [[Essence]]

## Profession / Category

- [[Open Essence]]

## Spell Grid

| Level | Spell | Area of Effect | Duration | Range | Type | Description |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Protection I | 1 target | `1 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | Subtracts `5` from elemental attack rolls against the target and adds `5` to the target's resistance rolls versus spells. |
| 2 | Cancel Essence | 1 spell | C | self | F | Forces incoming Essence spells targeting the caster to make a resistance roll first; on failure, the incoming spell has no effect on the caster. |
| 3 | Protection Sphere I | `10'R` | `1 min/lvl` | self | U | As Protection I, but all beings within `10'` radius of the target receive the benefits. |
| 4 | Cancel Mentalism | 1 spell | C | self | F | As Cancel Essence, but affects Mentalism spells. |
| 5 | Protection II | 1 target | `1 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | As Protection I, but the bonuses and penalties are `10`. |
| 6 | Cancel Channeling | 1 spell | C | self | F | As Cancel Essence, but affects Channeling spells. |
| 7 | Protection Sphere II | `10'R` | `1 min/lvl` | self | U | As Protection II, but affects a `10'` radius as in Protection Sphere I. |
| 8 | Dispel Essence Sphere I | `10'R` | C | self | F | As Cancel Essence, but affects an area centered on the caster; existing spells in the radius must resist with `+30` or be cancelled. |
| 9 | Essence Shield | caster | C | self | U | Creates a shield that subtracts `10` from frontal Essence attack rolls and can parry one spell attack if the caster takes no other action that round. |
| 10 | Mind Shield | caster | C | self | U | As Essence Shield, but effective only against Mentalism spells. |
