# Elemental Shields

## Summary

Open Essence protective spells against light, heat, cold, electricity, and elemental attack forms.

## Realm

- [[Essence]]

## Profession / Category

- [[Open Essence]]

## Spell Grid

| Level | Spell | Area of Effect | Duration | Range | Type | Description |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Resist Light | 1 target | `1 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | Protects against natural light effects and grants `+10` to resistance rolls versus light/electricity and `+10` DB against elemental electricity attacks. |
| 2 | Resist Heat | 1 target | `1 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | As Resist Light, but protects against natural heat to `200°F` and applies the modifiers to heat. |
| 3 | Resist Cold | 1 target | `1 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | As Resist Light, but protects against natural cold to `-20°F` and applies the modifiers to cold. |
| 4 | Resist Light Sphere | `10'R` | `1 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | As Resist Light, but protects all beings within `10'` radius of the target. |
| 5 | Resist Heat Sphere | `10'R` | `1 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | As Resist Heat, but protects all beings within `10'` radius of the target. |
| 6 | Resist Cold Sphere | `10'R` | `1 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | As Resist Cold, but protects all beings within `10'` radius of the target. |
| 7 | No spell listed | - | - | - | - | No spell is listed at this level in the source. |
| 8 | Lightarmor | 1 target | `1 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | As Resist Light, but protects against all natural light and electricity, with `+20` to resistance rolls and DB. |
| 9 | Heatarmor | 1 target | `1 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | As Lightarmor, but protects against heat. |
| 10 | Coldarmor | 1 target | `1 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | As Lightarmor, but protects against cold. |

## Special Notes

- No spells on this list are cumulative with each other.
