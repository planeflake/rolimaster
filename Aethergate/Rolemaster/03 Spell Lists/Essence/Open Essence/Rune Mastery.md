## Special Notes

- Rune paper is specially prepared to hold enchantments. After a rune is read, it fades from the paper, though the paper may hold another rune.
- A rune's attack level is the level of the inscribed spell. Elemental attack runes use the reader's appropriate Directed Spell OB.
