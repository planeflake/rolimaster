# Lesser Illusions

## Summary

Open Essence illusion spells for ventriloquism, mirages, glamours, phantasms, and delayed illusions.

## Realm

- [[Essence]]

## Profession / Category

- [[Open Essence]]

## Spell Grid

| Level | Spell | Area of Effect | Duration | Range | Type | Description |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Ventriloquism | 1 point | C | `100'` | E | Makes the caster's voice appear to come from any visible point within range. |
| 2 | Sound/Light Mirage | `10'R` | `10 min/lvl` | `100'` | E | Creates a simple immobile image, scene, sound, or set of sounds within a `10'` radius. |
| 3 | Taste/Smell Mirage | `10'R` | `10 min/lvl` | `100'` | E | As Sound/Light Mirage, but creates immobile smells or tastes in the area. |
| 4 | Illusions II | `10'R` | `1 min/lvl` | `100'` | E | Creates a simple immobile image or scene with one enhancement such as added sense, doubled duration, doubled range, or doubled radius. |
| 5 | Phantasm I | `10'R` | `1 min/lvl(C)` | `100'` | E | Creates the image of one object or being that moves while the caster concentrates and stops when concentration stops. |
| 6 | Light Glamour | 1 object | `1 day/lvl` | `50'` | E | Changes the visual appearance of one object up to `10 lb/lvl`. |
| 7 | Waiting Illusion II | `10'R` | varies | `50'` | E | As Illusion II, but delayed up to 24 hours and triggered by time, movement, sound, smell, or taste. |
| 8 | Illusions III | `10'R` | `1 min/lvl` | `100'` | E | As Illusions II, but two enhancement options may be chosen. |
| 9 | Phantasm II | `10'R` | `1 min/lvl(C)` | `100'` | E | As Phantasm I, but one enhancement option may be added, such as another sense, increased duration/range/radius, another image, or repetitive additions. |
| 10 | Waiting Phantasm II | `10'R` | varies | `100'` | E | As Phantasm II, but delayed like Waiting Illusion II; the phantasm may move, act on a simple command, or deliver a short speech if sound is included. |

## Special Notes

- If more than one option can be added to an Illusion or Phantasm spell, the same option may be chosen more than once.
- Sense aspects of these illusions are actually created; detecting the illusion normally requires magic or a conflicting sense.
