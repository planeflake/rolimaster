# Physical Enhancement

## Summary

Open Essence enhancement spells for scale, hearing, balance, vision, voice, water breathing, and gas breathing.

## Realm

- [[Essence]]

## Profession / Category

- [[Open Essence]]

## Spell Grid

| Level | Spell | Area of Effect | Duration | Range | Type | Description |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Mannish Scale | 1 object | - | `1'` | I | Tells the caster an object's exact dimensions, mass, and/or weight. |
| 2 | Sly Ears | 1 target | `10 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | Doubles the target's normal hearing and grants bonuses to awareness skills involving hearing. |
| 3 | Balance | 1 target | varies | `10'` | U | Grants `+50` to maneuver rolls for slow maneuvers requiring balance. |
| 4 | Nightvision | 1 target | `10 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | Lets the target see `100'` on a normal night as if it were daylight. |
| 5 | Sidevision | 1 target | `10 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | Gives the target a `300 degree` field of vision, lowering flank and rear attack bonuses. |
| 6 | Sounding | 1 target | `10 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | Triples the target's voice loudness without affecting spell-casting capability. |
| 7 | Watervision | 1 target | `10 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | As Nightvision, but allows `100'` sight through any water, including muddy or murky water. |
| 8 | Waterlungs | 1 target | `10 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | Allows the target to breathe water but not air. |
| 9 | Heatvision | 1 target | `10 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | As Nightvision, but sees varying degrees of heat on surfaces; it does not see through things or support detailed reading/mapping. |
| 10 | Gaslungs | 1 target | `10 min/lvl` | `10'` | U | As Waterlungs, but the target can breathe any gas as normal air. |
