# Unbarring Ways

## Summary

Open Essence spells for locking, magical locks, lock and trap lore, opening, disarming, jamming, weakening, and prison opening.

## Realm

- [[Essence]]

## Profession / Category

- [[Open Essence]]

## Spell Grid

| Level | Spell | Area of Effect | Duration | Range | Type | Description |
|---:|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | Lock | 1 lock | - | `100'` | U | Locks any visible lock within range as a normal lock, which can still be opened normally. |
| 2 | Magic Lock | 1 door | `1 min/lvl` | touch | U | Magically locks a door or container; it can be broken down or dispelled, but otherwise cannot be opened during the duration. |
| 3 | Locklore | 1 lock | - | touch | I | Grants the caster `+20` to pick the analyzed lock; someone instructed from the information gains `+10`. |
| 4 | Opening I | 1 lock | - | touch | U | Gives a `20%` chance to open a normal lock and `45%` chance to open a Magic Lock, with a chance to set off associated traps. |
| 5 | Traplore | 1 trap | - | touch | I | As Locklore, but applies to disarming traps. |
| 6 | Disarm I | 1 trap | - | touch | U | As Opening I, but disarms traps; there is a chance the trap is set off. |
| 7 | Jamming | 1 door | P | `50'` | U | Causes a door to expand and jam into its frame, with severity determined by roll. |
| 8 | Weakening | 1 door | P | `50'` | U | Reduces a door's inherent strength by `50%`; attempts to break it gain `+50`. |
| 9 | Open Prison I | `5'R/lvl` | P | self | U | Gives a `20%` chance to unlock all locks in the area, or `45%` for Magic Locks, rolling separately for each lock. |
| 10 | Opening II | 1 lock | - | touch | U | As Opening I, but chance is `40%`, or `90%` for Magic Locks. |

## Special Notes

- Locks and traps have difficulty modifiers. Spell success chances are modified by half the relevant difficulty modifier.
