# Symbolism

## Summary

Permanent magical trap-symbols emplaced on stone, surfaces, or living creatures. Symbols store spells that trigger automatically on defined conditions. Companion list to [[Inscriptions]] (for rune paper and signs).

## Notes

1. Caster must have at least as many ranks of Symbol Lore as the level of the Symbol spell they wish to cast or bypass. At least half as many ranks are required to Unsymbol.
2. See Spell Law Section 2.2.10 for specific rules on Symbols.
3. Normally, a Symbol that affects a being or thing can be triggered only once per day. If focused on a location (not the triggering person), OB is +50 instead of +0.
