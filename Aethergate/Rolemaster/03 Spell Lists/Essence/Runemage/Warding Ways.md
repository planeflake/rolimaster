# Warding Ways

## Summary

Ward magic for the Runemage. Creates magical barriers that block creatures, undead, demons, or magic realms from entering or passing through an area. Requires prepared ward signs inscribed at the centre or endpoints of the warded area.

## Notes

1. All wards require the caster to prepare the appropriate ward sign at the centre of the area of effect, or at either end of a line of effect. Ward signs are magical designs inscribed on any surface.
2. A single ward sign creates an area equal to 1'R per caster level. Two ward signs create an effect between them, up to 2' per caster level apart.
3. To create a ward sign, the caster must have an equal number of ranks in Ward Lore (Lore » Magical category) as the level of the ward spell, and must spend 1 round per ward level in its creation.
