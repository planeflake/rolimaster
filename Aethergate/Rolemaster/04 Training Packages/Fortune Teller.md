# Fortune Teller

## Summary

Often self-taught, Fortune Tellers earn a living reassuring anxious clients about the future or entertaining friends. Success depends as much on reading the querent as on true ability.

## Acquisition

- **Type:** Vocational (V)
- **Time to acquire:** 40 months
- **Starting money:** Normal
