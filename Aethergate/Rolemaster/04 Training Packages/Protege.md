# Protege

## Summary

The sole or star pupil of a learned spell user. A Protege gains deep knowledge of a chosen magical area and a lasting world-view shaped by service to a mentor.

## Acquisition

- **Type:** Lifestyle (L)
- **Time to acquire:** 105 months
- **Starting money:** Normal

## Special Benefits

| Benefit | Target |
|---|---:|
| Friends with an ally of mentor | 30 |
| Powerful enemy or rival | 30 |
| Book (`+10` non-magical bonus to a specific lore skill) | 50 |
| Potion (one dose, 5th-level spell) | 50 |
| Daily I item (3rd-level spell) | 40 |
| Secret knowledge | 30 |
| Spell adder (`+1`) | 40 |
| Spell adder (`+2`) | 20 |
| Spell multiplier (`x2`) | 5 |
| Notes | 0 |

The last benefit is always taken. The notes are a complete copy of a special spell researched by the mentor; the GM determines the spell.

## Skill Ranks

| Category or skill | Ranks |
|---|---:|
| Lore - General skill category | 2 |
| Choice of up to two general lore skills | 2 total |
| Lore - Magical skill category | 3 |
| Choice of up to two magical lore skills | 3 total |
| Power Awareness skill category | 2 |
| Choice of up to two power-awareness skills | 2 total |
| Power Manipulation skill category | 0 |
| Spell Mastery in one spell list | 2 |
| Choice of one other power-manipulation skill | 2 |
| Spells - Own Realm TP skill category | N/A |
| Choice of two or three spell lists | 6 total |

## Requirements

- **Spell-list note:** Lists are chosen with GM consent and are usually selected from those normally available to the character. Training-package lists attached to specific packages should not be granted.
- **Professional qualifier:** None.
- **Lifestyle skills:** None.

## Stat Gains

One realm stat.
