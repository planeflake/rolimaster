# Demonologist

## Summary

A demonologist specializes in dealing with demons. Continued contact leaves its mark, and demonologists eventually succumb to the evil influences with which they bargain.

## Acquisition

- **Type:** Lifestyle (L)
- **Time to acquire:** 132 months
- **Starting money:** Normal
