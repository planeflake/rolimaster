# Adventurer

## Summary

A broad package for characters who seek fortune through travel, exploration, and personal risk.

## Acquisition

- **Type:** Lifestyle (L)
- **Time to acquire:** 24 months
- **Starting money:** Normal plus `d10` open-ended
