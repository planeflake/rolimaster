# Animal Handler

## Summary

Animal handlers keep, groom, raise, breed, heal, and train animals or other creatures. They are often employed by Corpist spell users, although some maintain animals for profit or as a hobby.

## Acquisition

- **Type:** Vocational (V)
- **Time to acquire:** 37 months
- **Starting money:** Normal
