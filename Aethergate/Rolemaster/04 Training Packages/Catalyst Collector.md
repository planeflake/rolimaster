# Catalyst Collector

## Summary

Catalyst collectors supply magical practitioners with spell catalysts. They seek out rare and dangerous materials for profit and often find employment with guilds and colleges.

## Acquisition

- **Type:** Vocational (V)
- **Time to acquire:** 37 months
- **Starting money:** Normal
