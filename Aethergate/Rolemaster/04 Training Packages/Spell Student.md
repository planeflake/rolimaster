# Spell Student

## Summary

A student taught by a collegium or school of magic. Spell students gain broad exposure to spell lists and magical theories, although their cloistered studies leave them short of practical experience.

## Acquisition

- **Type:** Vocational (V)
- **Time to acquire:** 105 months
- **Starting money:** Normal

## Requirements

- **Spell-list note:** May choose either Guildcraft Mastery or Traveler's Ways as a TP spell list.
- **Professional qualifier:** Total temporary Memory and Reasoning stats of `180+` (`-5` points).
- **Lifestyle skills:** None.
