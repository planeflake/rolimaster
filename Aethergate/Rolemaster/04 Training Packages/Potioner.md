# Potioner

## Summary

A magical apothecary who uses spells to craft potions of healing, magic, and poison, drawing the Essence from natural herbs and capturing it in bottles.

## Acquisition

- **Type:** Lifestyle (L)
- **Time to acquire:** 39 months
- **Starting money:** Normal
