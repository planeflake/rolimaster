# Dreamweaver

## Summary

The Dreamweaver taps the mind through dreams, selecting and manipulating dreams for divination, wish fulfillment, creativity, or influence over others.

## Acquisition

- **Type:** Lifestyle (L)
- **Time to acquire:** 124 months
- **Starting money:** Normal

## Special Benefits

| Benefit | Target |
|---|---:|
| `d10` stored daydream patterns | 50 |

| `d5` stored nightmare patterns | 20 |
| `d10` stored dreamer patterns of family or associates (GM choice) | 20 |
| Book on dreams (`+10` to Dream Lore) | 0 |
