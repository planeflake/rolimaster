# Physician

## Summary

A professional healer trained in diagnosis and treatment of injury and illness. Physicians are commonly bound by duties to patients and may serve noble families or maintain city practices.

## Acquisition

- **Type:** Lifestyle (L)
- **Time to acquire:** 147 months
- **Starting money:** Normal plus open-ended `d10`
