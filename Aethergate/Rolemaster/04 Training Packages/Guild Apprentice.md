# Guild Apprentice

## Summary

A guild apprentice studies magic under a mercantile group of magicians or an established guild member. Their education prepares them for a magical career in a city.

## Acquisition

- **Type:** Lifestyle (L)
- **Time to acquire:** 86 months
- **Starting money:** Normal
