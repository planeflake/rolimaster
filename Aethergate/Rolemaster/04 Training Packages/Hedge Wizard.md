# Hedge Wizard

## Summary

A primitive Essence user who learns through oral tradition and practice rather than dusty books. Hedge wizards excel at ritual magic but find spell lists more difficult to learn.

## Acquisition

- **Type:** Lifestyle (L)

- **Starting money:** Normal
