# Plains Culture

## Summary

Plains cultures belong to grasslands, steppe, savanna, prairie, and open farming or herding regions. Their lives are shaped by distance, weather, animals, crops, raids, trade routes, and the visibility of everything under the sky.

## Environment

- Grasslands, open farms, herding ranges, river valleys, wind-scoured plains, and caravan roads.
- Communities may be nomadic, settled, urban, or a mix of seasonal herding and permanent markets.
- Horses, herd animals, wagons, weather, and long-distance travel are central concerns.

## Clothing and Decoration

- Practical riding or farming clothes, boots, cloaks, hats, wraps, leather, wool, and durable cloth are common.
- Decoration may include beadwork, embroidery, horse gear, clan marks, feathers, trade metal, or charms against storm and drought.
- Clothing must handle wind, dust, rain, sun, and riding.

## Attitudes and Dispositions

- Plains folk often value hospitality, mobility, courage, animal skill, memory, and respect for open land.
- They may distrust enclosed places, dense forests, or people who cannot read weather.
- Common fears include drought, wildfire, storms, famine, raids, and the loss of herds.

## Professions and Hobbies

- The culture suits riders, herders, scouts, farmers, traders, hunters, archers, priests, and messengers.
- Common skill themes include riding, animal handling, animal training, weather watching, survival, foraging, weapon training, regional lore, tracking, and trade.

## Background Languages

- The exact language choices should be transcribed with the rank table pass.
- For Mana World, plains cultures should include trade speech, clan dialects, horse-herder cants, and neighboring regional languages.

## Outfitting Options

- Riding animals, tack, bows, spears, knives, light armor, tents, wagons, trade goods, herd markers, and weatherproof travel supplies.
- Urban plains characters may replace riding gear with craft tools, merchant goods, or city weapons.

## Subcultures

- Plains Nomadic.
- Plains Urban.
- Plains Urban Upper Class.
- Plains Urban Middle Class.
- Plains Urban Underclass.

## Character Hooks

- Horse messenger, cattle guard, weather prophet, caravan scout, herd-heir, grassland archer, city-born trader, or survivor of a burned settlement.

## Mana World Notes

Plains cultures can read mana through storm lines, migration paths, crop circles, old road shrines, and places where the horizon reveals font-light.
