# Ratio Office

## Overview

The Ratio Office is the civic bureau that calculates mana rationing, storage rotation, conversion schedules, decay risk, and emergency priority during shortages.

Its clerks decide which districts dim, which factories pause, which reservoirs are spent, and when [[Mana Conversion]] becomes necessary.

Everyone needs the Ratio Office.

Everyone hates the Ratio Office.

## Duties

- Track city mana reserves by realm and grade
- Forecast [[Mana Decay]]
- Authorize conversion at [[The Convergence Works]]
- Protect minimum pressure for [[The Heartline]]
- Schedule [[The Day of Open Valves]]
- Recommend or resist [[Violet Protocol]]
- Publish public rationing notices

## Reputation

The office is seen as cold, mathematical, and necessary.

Its defenders say the city survives because someone is willing to count what others only pray over or spend.

Its enemies say no ledger should decide whether shrines dim, hospitals live, or districts go dark.

## Campaign Uses

- A Ratio Office clerk quietly hires the party because the public numbers do not match the real reserves.
- Protesters demand the office release conversion records.
- A forged decay forecast is used to justify an unnecessary Day of Open Valves.
- The office knows Arcane conversion is coming before the Mana Authority admits it.
