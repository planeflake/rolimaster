# White Line Wardens

## Overview

The White Line Wardens are a specialized city guard unit sworn to protect [[The Heartline]], hospital valves, converter halls, emergency reservoirs, and critical Essence transports.

Most citizens call them the Mercy Guard.

Their main city post is [[White Line Barracks]].

## Duties

- Guard exposed Heartline valves
- Escort emergency mana canisters
- Protect repair crews during outages
- Secure converter stations during protests
- Arrest siphon crews and sabotage cells
- Seal Underworks routes when the hospital feed is threatened
- Enforce hospital priority during rationing

## Equipment

- White-enamel armbands or breastplates
- Copper pressure masks
- Resonance lanterns that reveal leaks and conversion residue
- Mana-shear polearms for cutting illegal siphon clamps
- Emergency seal-runes keyed to hospital engineers
- Portable pressure bells tuned to Essence flow

## Reputation

The Wardens are respected, resented, and feared.

They protect the sick, but they also enforce the hard arithmetic of rationing. When they arrive at a valve station, someone else is probably about to lose pressure.

## Current Tension

The Wardens are stretched thin by minor mana thefts, religious protests against [[Mana Conversion]], and rumors that illegal arrays are operating near Cogmarket.

## Campaign Uses

- The party is deputized to help guard a Heartline repair crew.
- A Warden commander hides evidence of converted Essence side effects to prevent panic.
- A sabotage cell steals Warden gear to reach a protected valve.
- The Wardens must choose between protecting a public festival and securing the hospital line.
