# Mana Authority

## Overview

The Mana Authority licenses, taxes, audits, and polices the legal movement of mana through [[Aethergate]].

Its inspectors track canisters, monitor reservoir quotas, certify [[Mana Absorption Arrays]], and investigate thefts that threaten the city's supply.

## Public Role

- License mana refiners and array operators
- Inspect liquid and gaseous mana canisters
- Audit district pressure reports
- Confiscate illegal inhalers and siphon devices
- Coordinate with the [[Guild of Conduits]]

## Current Problem

The Authority is treating the recent losses as ordinary theft because ordinary theft has paperwork, suspects, and fines.

If mana is being removed before it reaches registered surface conduits, the city has a deeper problem than smuggling.
